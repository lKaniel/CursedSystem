# Basic Actions and Conditions

A comprehensive reference for actions available to all characters and conditions that can affect them.

## Basic Actions

These actions are available to every character without needing special abilities.

### Movement Actions

#### Walk/Run
**Cost:** 1 SP per 5 feet
**Effect:** Move normally across the battlefield
**Details:**
- Standard movement speed
- Can be split (move-attack-move)
- Doesn't provoke opportunity attacks when moving normally

#### Sprint
**Cost:** 1 SP per 5 feet (normal) + additional 5 SP activation cost
**Effect:** Double your movement speed for this turn
**Details:**
- Must use Sprint at the start of your turn
- Example: Normal 30 feet becomes 60 feet
- Total cost: 5 SP (activation) + 12 SP (60 feet) = 17 SP
- Can't Sprint if wearing Heavy Armor without Might 15+

#### Jump
**Cost:** 2 SP per 5 feet jumped
**Effect:** Leap horizontally or vertically
**Details:**
- **Horizontal Jump:** Can jump up to (Might/2) feet horizontally with running start
- **Vertical Jump:** Can jump up to (Might/4) feet vertically
- **Standing Jump:** Half distance, same cost
- Must have sufficient movement remaining
- Landing in difficult terrain or on uneven surface may require Acrobatics check

#### Climb
**Cost:** 2 SP per 5 feet climbed
**Effect:** Scale walls, trees, ropes, etc.
**Details:**
- Requires handholds or climbing equipment
- May require Athletics check for difficult surfaces
- DC depends on surface:
  - Easy (rope, ladder): No check
  - Medium (tree, rough wall): DC 12
  - Hard (smooth stone): DC 18
  - Very Hard (glass, ice): DC 25+
- Failure: Fall and take falling damage

#### Swim
**Cost:** 2 SP per 5 feet swum
**Effect:** Move through water
**Details:**
- Requires Athletics check if in rough water
- Calm water: No check
- Rough water: DC 15
- Storm/rapids: DC 20+
- Wearing Heavy Armor: Disadvantage or can't swim

#### Crawl (While Prone)
**Cost:** 2 SP per 5 feet
**Effect:** Move while prone
**Details:**
- Half normal speed
- Often used to stay in cover
- Ranged attacks against you have disadvantage
- Melee attacks against you have advantage

#### Stand Up (From Prone)
**Cost:** 3 SP
**Effect:** Rise from prone to standing
**Details:**
- Requires 3 SP
- Can't stand if grappled or restrained
- May provoke opportunity attacks depending on DM ruling

#### Squeeze
**Cost:** 2 SP per 5 feet
**Effect:** Move through spaces smaller than your size
**Details:**
- Can squeeze through space one size smaller (Medium â†’ Small = 2.5 feet)
- Move at half speed (costs double)
- Disadvantage on attack rolls and Agility saves
- Attackers have advantage against you

### Combat Actions

#### Basic Attack (Melee)
**Cost:**
- Heavy Melee: 4 SP
- Light Melee: 3 SP
- Unarmed: 2 SP

**Effect:** Make a weapon attack against a target within reach
**Details:**
- Roll: d(Stat/2 + Skill/2) + (Stat/2 + Skill/2)
- Compare to target's Defense Class
- Deal weapon damage on hit

#### Basic Attack (Ranged)
**Cost:** 3 SP
**Effect:** Make a ranged weapon attack
**Details:**
- Must have ammunition
- Disadvantage if enemy within 5 feet
- Range limits apply (short/long range)
- Long range: Disadvantage on attack

#### Grapple
**Cost:** 4 SP
**Effect:** Grab and restrain a creature
**Details:**
- Target must be no more than one size larger
- Contested: Your (Might + Athletics) vs their (Might + Athletics) or (Agility + Acrobatics)
- Success: Target is grappled (speed 0, restrained)
- Must have one free hand
- You can move grappled target at half speed

#### Shove
**Cost:** 4 SP
**Effect:** Push a creature or knock them prone
**Details:**
- Target must be no more than one size larger
- Contested: Your (Might + Athletics) vs their (Might + Athletics) or (Agility + Acrobatics)
- Success: Push 5 feet OR knock prone
- Can shove into hazards (off cliffs, into fire, etc.)

#### Disarm
**Cost:** 5 SP
**Effect:** Knock weapon from target's hand
**Details:**
- Contested: Your attack roll vs their weapon skill roll
- Success: They drop their weapon at their feet
- They can pick it up (costs 2 SP as interaction)

#### Escape Grapple
**Cost:** 4 SP
**Effect:** Break free from a grapple
**Details:**
- Contested: Your (Might + Athletics) or (Agility + Acrobatics) vs grappler's (Might + Athletics)
- Success: You're no longer grappled

#### Dodge
**Cost:** 4 SP
**Trigger:** Being targeted by an attack
**Effect:** Impose disadvantage on one attack against you
**Details:**
- Can be used as reaction
- Attacker rolls twice, takes lower result
- Only affects one attack

#### Help
**Cost:** 3 SP
**Effect:** Aid an ally with their action
**Details:**
- Choose one ally and one task
- They gain advantage on their next ability check or attack
- Must be able to meaningfully assist
- Example: Help ally attack (flank, distract) or help with skill check

#### Ready Action
**Cost:** No cost to ready, costs normal amount when triggered
**Effect:** Prepare an action for a specific trigger
**Details:**
- Declare trigger: "If X happens, I do Y"
- When trigger occurs, you can use your reaction to do the action
- If trigger doesn't occur, nothing happens
- Example: "If enemy comes through door, I attack"

### Utility Actions

#### Hide
**Cost:** 3 SP
**Effect:** Attempt to become hidden
**Details:**
- Requires cover or obscurement
- Roll: Agility + Stealth vs observers' Awareness + Perception
- Success: You are hidden (attackers have disadvantage, you have advantage)
- Failure: You remain visible
- Breaking stealth: Attacking, casting loud spell, or moving into open

#### Search
**Cost:** 3 SP
**Effect:** Look for hidden creatures, objects, or clues
**Details:**
- Roll: Awareness + Perception or Awareness + Investigation
- DC set by DM based on how hidden the thing is
- Can search a 10-foot area or look for one specific thing

#### Use Object/Interact
**Cost:** 2 SP
**Effect:** Manipulate an object
**Details:**
- Open/close door
- Draw/sheathe weapon
- Pick up item
- Drink potion
- Pull lever
- Most simple interactions

#### Dash Through
**Cost:** 3 SP + movement cost
**Effect:** Run through an enemy's space
**Details:**
- Contested: Your (Agility + Acrobatics) vs their (Agility + Acrobatics)
- Success: Move through their space without stopping
- Failure: Movement ends, you're in their reach, may provoke opportunity attack
- Can't dash through creatures 2+ sizes larger

### Social Actions

#### Persuade
**Cost:** 3 SP (in tense/combat situations)
**Effect:** Convince someone to do something
**Details:**
- Roll: Presence + Persuasion vs their Awareness + Insight
- Success: They're inclined to help or believe you
- Can't make them do something obviously harmful

#### Deceive
**Cost:** 3 SP (in tense situations)
**Effect:** Tell a convincing lie
**Details:**
- Roll: Presence + Deception vs their Awareness + Insight
- Success: They believe you
- Critical failure: They know you're lying and may become hostile

#### Intimidate
**Cost:** 4 SP
**Effect:** Frighten or coerce with threats
**Details:**
- Roll: Presence + Physical Intimidation OR Might + Physical Intimidation
- Target contests with Awareness + Insight
- Success: They're frightened or comply with demand
- Doesn't work on creatures immune to fear

#### Performance
**Cost:** 3 SP (for quick performance)
**Effect:** Entertain, distract, or captivate
**Details:**
- Roll: Presence + Performance
- Can create distractions (allies get advantage on Stealth)
- Can earn money in towns
- Can sway crowds

### Advanced Movement

#### Long Jump (Running)
**Cost:** 2 SP per 5 feet jumped
**Maximum Distance:** Might/2 feet
**Details:**
- Requires 10-foot running start
- Example: Might 12 = can jump 6 feet (costs 4 SP)
- Must have movement remaining
- Failing to clear gap = fall

#### Long Jump (Standing)
**Cost:** 2 SP per 5 feet jumped
**Maximum Distance:** Might/4 feet
**Details:**
- No running start needed
- Half the distance of running jump

#### High Jump
**Cost:** 2 SP per 5 feet jumped
**Maximum Height:** Might/4 feet
**Details:**
- Vertical leap to reach high objects
- Example: Might 16 = can jump 4 feet high (costs 4 SP)
- Can grab ledge at maximum height

#### Drop Prone
**Cost:** 0 SP
**Effect:** Fall flat on the ground
**Details:**
- Instant, can be done anytime
- Useful for avoiding attacks or taking cover
- Ranged attacks against you have disadvantage
- Melee attacks against you have advantage
- Must spend 3 SP to stand up

#### Tumble
**Cost:** 5 SP
**Effect:** Acrobatically move through enemy space
**Details:**
- Requires successful Agility + Acrobatics check
- DC = 10 + enemy's Agility
- Success: Move through without provoking opportunity attack
- Failure: Movement stops, normal opportunity attack rules

### Mounted Actions

#### Mount/Dismount
**Cost:** 3 SP
**Effect:** Get on or off a mount
**Details:**
- Mount must be willing or trained
- Can't mount if grappled or restrained

#### Control Mount
**Cost:** 2 SP
**Effect:** Command your mount to move or act
**Details:**
- Trained mounts act on your initiative
- Untrained require Animal Handling check

### Improvised Actions

Characters can attempt any reasonable action not listed here. The DM sets:
- Stamina/Mana/HP cost (typically 3-5 SP for unusual actions)
- Difficulty Class
- Relevant stat + skill check

**Examples:**
- Break down door: Might + Athletics, DC 20, 5 SP
- Balance on rope: Agility + Acrobatics, DC 15, 3 SP per turn
- Identify spell being cast: Intellect + Arcana, DC 15, 2 SP reaction

## Conditions

Conditions alter a creature's capabilities in various ways.

### Blinded
**Effect:**
- Automatically fail any check requiring sight
- Attack rolls have disadvantage
- Attack rolls against you have advantage
- Can't use Perception for visual searching

**Common Sources:**
- Darkness spell
- Blindness spell
- Thrown sand/dirt
- Environmental effects (smoke, fog)

**How to Remove:**
- Spell ends
- Wash eyes (1 minute, uses Short Rest)
- Healing magic
- Wait for duration to expire

### Charmed
**Effect:**
- Can't attack the charmer
- Charmer has advantage on social checks against you
- You regard the charmer as a friendly acquaintance

**Does NOT:**
- Make you obey commands
- Make you risk your life
- Override strong beliefs

**Common Sources:**
- Charm Person spell
- Vampire's charm
- Fey magic
- High Looks + Presence abilities

**How to Remove:**
- Duration expires
- Charmer harms you (breaks immediately)
- Charm removal spell
- Strong emotional shock

### Deafened
**Effect:**
- Automatically fail any check requiring hearing
- Can't hear verbal communication
- Disadvantage on initiative (lose Awareness bonus)

**Common Sources:**
- Thunderwave spell
- Loud explosion
- Silence spell (in area)

**How to Remove:**
- Duration expires
- Lesser Restoration
- Wait 1d4 hours (for temporary deafness)

### Frightened
**Effect:**
- Disadvantage on all ability checks and attack rolls while source of fear is visible
- Can't willingly move closer to source of fear
- Can still move away or sideways

**Common Sources:**
- Fear spell
- Dragon's Frightful Presence
- Intimidation check
- Horror effects

**How to Remove:**
- Move out of range
- Duration expires
- Calm Emotions spell
- Can attempt save each turn (if effect allows)

### Grappled
**Effect:**
- Speed becomes 0
- Can't benefit from movement bonuses
- Condition ends if grappler is incapacitated

**Does NOT:**
- Prevent attacking
- Prevent casting spells
- Give disadvantage on attacks

**Common Sources:**
- Grapple action
- Tentacles
- Entangle spell
- Quicksand

**How to Remove:**
- Escape (4 SP, contested check)
- Grappler releases you
- Grappler becomes incapacitated
- Teleport

### Incapacitated
**Effect:**
- Can't take actions, reactions, or use stamina
- Can still move (unless otherwise stated)

**Common Sources:**
- Stunned condition
- Paralyzed condition
- Petrified condition
- Being unconscious

**How to Remove:**
- Depends on source
- Usually when other condition ends

### Invisible
**Effect:**
- Can't be seen without special senses
- Attack rolls have advantage
- Attack rolls against you have disadvantage
- Can still be heard, smelled, tracked

**Common Sources:**
- Invisibility spell
- Greater Invisibility
- Special items
- Creature abilities

**How to Remove:**
- Duration expires
- You attack or cast spell (unless Greater Invisibility)
- Dispel Magic
- See Invisibility spell

### Paralyzed
**Effect:**
- Incapacitated (can't take actions or reactions)
- Can't move or speak
- Automatically fail Might and Agility saves
- Attack rolls against you have advantage
- Attacks from within 5 feet are automatic criticals

**Very dangerous condition!**

**Common Sources:**
- Hold Person spell
- Ghoul's paralyzing touch
- Poison effects
- Lightning Strike (critical)

**How to Remove:**
- Duration expires
- Save at end of turn (if allowed)
- Lesser Restoration
- Ally breaks concentration (if from spell)

### Petrified
**Effect:**
- Transformed to stone (or other solid substance)
- Incapacitated
- Can't move or speak
- Unaware of surroundings
- Resistance to all damage
- Immune to poison and disease
- Don't age

**Weight increases by factor of 10.**

**Common Sources:**
- Flesh to Stone spell
- Basilisk gaze
- Medusa gaze
- Cockatrice touch

**How to Remove:**
- Greater Restoration
- Wish
- Special anti-petrification items

### Poisoned
**Effect:**
- Disadvantage on attack rolls
- Disadvantage on ability checks

**Does NOT:**
- Deal damage by itself (though poison source might)
- Prevent movement

**Common Sources:**
- Poisoned weapons
- Poison gas
- Creature bites (snake, spider)
- Spoiled food

**How to Remove:**
- Duration expires
- Lesser Restoration
- Antidote/Antitoxin (100 gold, cures most poisons)
- Successful Fortitude save (if ongoing)

### Prone
**Effect:**
- Only movement option is crawling (2 SP per 5 feet)
- Disadvantage on attack rolls
- Melee attacks against you have advantage
- Ranged attacks against you have disadvantage

**Useful defensively against ranged attackers!**

**Common Sources:**
- Shove action
- Trip attacks
- Falling
- Ice/slippery surfaces
- Voluntary (drop prone)

**How to Remove:**
- Stand up (3 SP)
- Forced movement can stand you up (DM discretion)

### Restrained
**Effect:**
- Speed becomes 0
- Disadvantage on attack rolls
- Disadvantage on Agility saves
- Attack rolls against you have advantage

**Worse than grappled!**

**Common Sources:**
- Entangle spell
- Net weapon
- Web spell
- Chains/ropes
- Quicksand

**How to Remove:**
- Escape check (varies by source)
- Break restraints (Might check)
- Ally cuts you free
- Spell ends

### Stunned
**Effect:**
- Incapacitated (can't take actions or reactions)
- Can't move
- Can speak only falteringly
- Automatically fail Might and Agility saves
- Attack rolls against you have advantage

**Common Sources:**
- Stunning Strike (monk ability)
- Stun spell
- Thunder damage (critical)
- Creature abilities

**How to Remove:**
- Duration expires (usually until end of attacker's next turn)
- Save at end of turn (if allowed)
- Very short duration usually

### Unconscious
**Effect:**
- Incapacitated
- Can't move or speak
- Unaware of surroundings
- Drop whatever you're holding
- Fall prone
- Automatically fail Might and Agility saves
- Attack rolls against you have advantage
- Attacks from within 5 feet are automatic criticals

**Extremely dangerous!**

**Common Sources:**
- Reduced to 0 HP
- Sleep spell
- Knockout poison
- Massive damage

**How to Remove:**
- Regain HP (if at 0)
- Duration expires
- Ally wakes you (1 SP action, slap/shake)
- Healing spell

### Exhaustion
**Special multi-level condition:**

Exhaustion is measured in levels (1-6).

| Level | Effect |
|-------|--------|
| 1 | Disadvantage on ability checks |
| 2 | Speed halved (movement costs double stamina) |
| 3 | Disadvantage on attack rolls and saves |
| 4 | HP maximum halved |
| 5 | Speed reduced to 0 (can't move) |
| 6 | Death |

**Effects stack!** (Level 3 = all effects of 1, 2, and 3)

**Common Sources:**
- No Long Rest for 24+ hours
- Forced march (8+ hours travel without rest)
- Extreme temperatures
- Starvation/dehydration
- Certain spells and abilities

**How to Remove:**
- Long Rest removes 1 level
- Greater Restoration removes 1 level
- Very slow to recover!

## Combining Conditions

Multiple conditions can affect a creature simultaneously:
- Effects stack unless they contradict
- Use the most severe effect when they conflict
- Example: Prone + Restrained = both apply
- Example: Invisible + Hidden = both apply (very hard to detect)

## Advantage and Disadvantage

Many conditions grant advantage or disadvantage.

**Advantage:** Roll 2 dice (or sets of dice), take the higher result
**Disadvantage:** Roll 2 dice (or sets of dice), take the lower result

**Important:** Advantage and disadvantage cancel out, regardless of how many sources.
- 1 advantage + 1 disadvantage = normal roll
- 5 advantages + 1 disadvantage = normal roll
