# Verdant Growth

## Description
Call upon nature's grasp, causing vines and roots to burst from the earth and entangle your foes.

## Prerequisites
- **Awareness:** 10
- **Survival:** 10

## Details
- **Type:** Nature Spell
- **Category:** [[Innate Magic]] - Nature (Plant)
- **Cost:** 7 MP + 3 SP
- **Cooldown:** None (Concentration, lasts 1 minute)
- **Range:** 90 feet (20-foot square area)

## Effect
Grasping plants and vines sprout from the ground in the area. The area becomes difficult terrain. Creatures in the area when cast or entering it must make a Might save or become restrained.

**Scaling:**
- Area size: 20-foot square base, +5 feet per 6 Survival (10→20ft, 16→25ft, 22→30ft, 28→35ft)
- Restrained creatures take 1d6 piercing damage at start of their turn at Survival 18+
- Damage increases: +1d6 per 10 Survival beyond 18

**Save DC:** 10 + (Awareness/2) + (Survival/2)

Restrained creatures can use an action to make a Might or Agility save to escape.

## Special
- Plants remain for 1 minute after concentration ends (as difficult terrain)
- Can be cast on any natural surface
- Ineffective on stone or metal floors

## Progression
- **Unlocks:** [[Thorn Barrier]], [[Strangling Vines]]
- **Prerequisite for:** [[Nature's Wrath]], [[Living Armor]]
