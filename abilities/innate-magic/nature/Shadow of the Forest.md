# Shadow of the Forest

## Description
Nature cloaks you and your allies in shadow and silence, making you nearly impossible to detect as you move.

## Prerequisites
- **Awareness:** 12
- **Survival:** 14
- **Stealth:** 10

## Details
- **Type:** Nature Spell
- **Category:** [[Innate Magic]] - Nature (Stealth)
- **Cost:** 11 MP + 3 SP
- **Cooldown:** None (Concentration, lasts 1 hour)
- **Range:** Self (30-foot aura)

## Effect
You and allies within 30 feet gain a massive bonus to Stealth checks and leave no physical tracks. Divination magic cannot locate you.

**Scaling:**
- Stealth bonus: +10 base, +(Survival/3) additional
  - Survival 14: +14 total
  - Survival 21: +17 total
  - Survival 30: +20 total
- At Survival 20+: Extend range to 60 feet
- At Survival 28+: Allies become invisible to darkvision

## Special
- You cannot be tracked by non-magical means
- Moving through natural terrain creates no sound
- Animals will not detect your presence unless you attack them
- Works even in broad daylight in natural settings

## Progression
- **Unlocks:** [[One with Nature]], [[Forest Walk]]
- **Prerequisite for:** [[Vanish]], [[Nature's Camouflage]]
