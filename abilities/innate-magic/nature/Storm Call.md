# Storm Call

## Description
Reach into the sky and command the fury of the storm, calling down bolts of lightning to smite your enemies.

## Prerequisites
- **Awareness:** 14
- **Survival:** 18

## Details
- **Type:** Nature Spell
- **Category:** [[Innate Magic]] - Nature (Weather)
- **Cost:** 16 MP + 4 SP
- **Cooldown:** None (Concentration, lasts up to 10 minutes)
- **Range:** 120 feet

## Effect
Storm clouds gather above you in a 60-foot radius. On each of your turns (including this one), you can use an action to call down a lightning bolt on one creature within range.

**Scaling:**
- Lightning damage: 3d10 base, +1d10 per 6 Survival (18→3d10, 24→4d10, 30→5d10, 36→6d10)
- At Survival 24+: Can strike two targets per turn
- At Survival 30+: Can strike three targets per turn
- At Survival 36+: Lightning can chain to one additional nearby creature (within 10 feet)

**Save DC:** 10 + (Awareness/2) + (Survival/2)
**On Success:** Half damage

## Special
- If outdoors in a storm, damage dice increase by one size (d10→d12)
- Area becomes lightly obscured by rain
- Loud thunder can be heard up to 300 feet away

## Progression
- **Requires:** Prior nature spell knowledge
- **Unlocks:** [[Hurricane]], [[Tempest Fury]]
- **Prerequisite for:** [[Apocalypse Storm]], [[Chain Lightning]]
