# Wild Restoration

## Description
Channel the regenerative power of nature through your touch, rapidly closing wounds and restoring vitality.

## Prerequisites
- **Awareness:** 10
- **Survival:** 10
- **Insight:** 6

## Details
- **Type:** Nature Spell
- **Category:** [[Innate Magic]] - Nature (Healing)
- **Cost:** 8 MP + 2 SP
- **Cooldown:** None
- **Range:** Touch or 60 feet (if using as bonus action for +3 SP)

## Effect
Touch a creature or speak a word of power to restore their health. Target regains hit points.

**Scaling:**
- Healing: 2d6 + (Awareness/2) base
- +1d6 per 8 Survival (10→2d6, 18→3d6, 26→4d6, 34→5d6)
- At Survival 20+: Also removes one poison or disease
- At Survival 30+: Can target two creatures simultaneously (costs +4 MP)

## Special
- Can be cast at range (60 feet) by spending additional 3 SP
- If target is at 0 HP, they regain consciousness
- Healing leaves faint green energy swirls on the target

## Progression
- **Unlocks:** [[Regeneration]], [[Mass Healing]]
- **Prerequisite for:** [[Life Link]], [[Circle of Renewal]]
