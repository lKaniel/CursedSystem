# Primal Shift

## Description
Your body flows like water, transforming into the shape of a beast as primal magic reshapes your form.

## Prerequisites
- **Awareness:** 14
- **Survival:** 20

## Details
- **Type:** Nature Spell (Transformation)
- **Category:** [[Innate Magic]] - Nature (Shapeshifting)
- **Cost:** 12 MP
- **Cooldown:** Per Encounter (lasts up to 1 hour or until dismissed)
- **Range:** Self

## Effect
Transform into a beast you've seen before. Your game statistics are replaced by the beast's, but you retain your Intellect, Awareness, and Presence scores. You can't cast spells in beast form.

**Scaling:**
- Maximum beast CR: Survival/5 (20→CR 4, 25→CR 5, 30→CR 6, 35→CR 7, 40→CR 8)
- At Survival 25+: Can speak while transformed
- At Survival 30+: Can cast spells with no material components while transformed
- At Survival 35+: Retain all mental abilities and can use simple tools

## Special
- You can revert to your normal form as a bonus action (costs 2 SP)
- Your equipment merges into the new form
- HP carries over between forms (damage doesn't reset)
- Can use this to scout, infiltrate, or gain specific animal abilities

## Beast Form Benefits
- Gain beast's physical stats, AC, movement speeds
- Gain beast's natural weapons and special abilities
- Can still use your skill proficiencies if the beast form allows

## Progression
- **Unlocks:** [[Elemental Form]], [[Chimera Shape]]
- **Prerequisite for:** [[Apex Predator]], [[True Polymorph]]
