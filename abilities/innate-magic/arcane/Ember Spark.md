# Ember Spark

## Description
Channel raw elemental heat into your palm and release it as a focused projectile of burning energy.

## Prerequisites
- **Intellect:** 8
- **Arcana:** 5

## Details
- **Type:** Arcane Cantrip
- **Category:** [[Innate Magic]] - Arcane (Fire)
- **Cost:** 0 MP (At-will) + 2 SP
- **Cooldown:** None
- **Range:** 90 feet

## Effect
Make a ranged spell attack against a target. On hit, deal fire damage.

**Scaling:** 1d8 base damage, +1d8 per 8 Arcana
- Arcana 5: 1d8 damage
- Arcana 13: 2d8 damage
- Arcana 21: 3d8 damage
- Arcana 29: 4d8 damage

**Attack Roll:** d(Intellect/2 + Arcana/2) + (Intellect/2 + Arcana/2) vs target's DC

## Progression
- **Unlocks:** [[Flame Surge]], [[Burning Sphere]]
- **Prerequisite for:** [[Heat Wave]], [[Inferno Blade]]
