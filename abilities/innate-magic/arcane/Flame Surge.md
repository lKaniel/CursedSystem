# Flame Surge

## Description
Ignite the air in front of you, creating a rushing wave of fire that engulfs everything in its path.

## Prerequisites
- **Intellect:** 10
- **Arcana:** 10

## Details
- **Type:** Arcane Spell
- **Category:** [[Innate Magic]] - Arcane (Fire)
- **Cost:** 6 MP + 3 SP
- **Cooldown:** None
- **Range:** Self (20-foot cone)

## Effect
All creatures in a 20-foot cone must make an Agility save or take fire damage.

**Scaling:** 2d8 base damage, +1d8 per 5 Arcana
- Arcana 10: 2d8 damage (9 avg)
- Arcana 15: 3d8 damage (13.5 avg)
- Arcana 20: 4d8 damage (18 avg)
- Arcana 25: 5d8 damage (22.5 avg)

**Save DC:** 10 + (Intellect/2) + (Arcana/2)
**On Success:** Half damage

## Progression
- **Requires:** [[Ember Spark]]
- **Unlocks:** [[Conflagration]], [[Wall of Flames]]
