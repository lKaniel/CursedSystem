# Arcane Barrier

## Description
Weave protective energy into an invisible shield that deflects incoming attacks.

## Prerequisites
- **Intellect:** 10
- **Arcana:** 7

## Details
- **Type:** Arcane Spell (Reaction)
- **Category:** [[Innate Magic]] - Arcane (Protection)
- **Cost:** 5 MP + 2 SP
- **Cooldown:** Reaction (when hit by attack)
- **Range:** Self

## Effect
When you are hit by an attack or targeted by a spell, you can create a magical barrier. Gain bonus AC until the start of your next turn. This may cause the triggering attack to miss.

**Scaling:**
- +5 AC base
- +1 AC per 8 Arcana (7→+5, 15→+6, 23→+7, 31→+8)
- At Arcana 20+: Also blocks 1d8 damage if attack still hits

## Special
- Can be activated after seeing the attack roll
- Works against both physical and magical attacks
- Visual: Shimmering hexagonal patterns appear in the air

## Progression
- **Unlocks:** [[Spell Reflection]], [[Mana Shield]]
- **Prerequisite for:** [[Prismatic Ward]], [[Impenetrable Fortress]]
