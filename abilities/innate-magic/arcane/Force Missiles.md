# Force Missiles

## Description
Manifest bolts of pure magical energy that unerringly strike your targets, never missing their mark.

## Prerequisites
- **Intellect:** 10
- **Arcana:** 8

## Details
- **Type:** Arcane Spell
- **Category:** [[Innate Magic]] - Arcane (Force)
- **Cost:** 7 MP + 3 SP
- **Cooldown:** None
- **Range:** 120 feet

## Effect
Create glowing missiles of force energy that automatically hit their targets. You can direct missiles at one target or spread them among multiple targets.

**Scaling:**
- Number of missiles: 3 base, +1 missile per 6 Arcana (8→3, 14→4, 20→5, 26→6, 32→7)
- Damage per missile: 1d6 force damage
- At Arcana 24+: Each missile deals 1d6+1 force damage
- At Arcana 36+: Each missile deals 1d6+2 force damage

## Special
- Missiles never miss (no attack roll needed)
- Can target creatures behind cover
- Cannot be dodged or parried
- Blocked only by total cover or specific anti-magic effects

## Progression
- **Unlocks:** [[Force Blade]], [[Kinetic Burst]]
- **Prerequisite for:** [[Arcane Barrage]], [[Disintegration Ray]]
