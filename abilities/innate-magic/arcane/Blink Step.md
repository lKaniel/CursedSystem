# Blink Step

## Description
Reality bends around you as you fold space, appearing in a new location in the blink of an eye.

## Prerequisites
- **Intellect:** 10
- **Arcana:** 12
- **Acrobatics:** 8

## Details
- **Type:** Arcane Spell (Teleportation)
- **Category:** [[Innate Magic]] - Arcane (Space)
- **Cost:** 8 MP + 2 SP
- **Cooldown:** None
- **Range:** Self

## Effect
Teleport yourself to any unoccupied space you can see within range. You can bring carried objects (up to your carrying capacity).

**Scaling:**
- Base range: 30 feet
- +10 feet per 8 Arcana (12→30ft, 20→40ft, 28→50ft, 36→60ft)
- At Arcana 25+: Can teleport as a reaction when targeted by an attack (costs +3 SP)

## Special
- Does not provoke opportunity attacks
- Can be cast while grappled (contest: your Arcana vs their Athletics)
- Can pass through barriers if you can see the destination

## Progression
- **Unlocks:** [[Phase Shift]], [[Dimensional Anchor]]
- **Prerequisite for:** [[Far Step]], [[Plane Walk]]
