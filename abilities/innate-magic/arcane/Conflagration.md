# Conflagration

## Description
Create a point of intense heat that violently erupts outward, consuming everything in a sphere of roaring flames.

## Prerequisites
- **Intellect:** 12
- **Arcana:** 18

## Details
- **Type:** Arcane Spell
- **Category:** [[Innate Magic]] - Arcane (Fire)
- **Cost:** 14 MP + 4 SP
- **Cooldown:** None
- **Range:** 150 feet (25-foot radius)

## Effect
Choose a point within range. All creatures within 25 feet of that point must make an Agility save or take fire damage. Objects ignite.

**Scaling:** 4d8 base damage, +1d8 per 6 Arcana
- Arcana 18: 4d8 damage (18 avg)
- Arcana 24: 5d8 damage (22.5 avg)
- Arcana 30: 6d8 damage (27 avg)
- Arcana 36: 7d8 damage (31.5 avg)

**Radius Scaling:** +5 feet per 12 Arcana
- Arcana 18: 25-foot radius
- Arcana 30: 30-foot radius
- Arcana 42: 35-foot radius

**Save DC:** 10 + (Intellect/2) + (Arcana/2)
**On Success:** Half damage

## Special
Ignites flammable objects not being worn or carried. Can detonate delayed explosive effects for bonus damage.

- **Requires:** [[Flame Surge]]
- **Unlocks:** [[Firestorm]], [[Phoenix Rebirth]]
