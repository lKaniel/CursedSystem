# Summon Familiar

## Description
Call forth a minor spirit that takes the form of a small creature, bonding with you as your magical companion.

## Prerequisites (to inscribe)
- **Intellect:** 10
- **Arcana:** 12
- **Awareness:** 8

## Details
- **Type:** Grimoire Spell (Conjuration Ritual)
- **Category:** [[Grimoire Magic]] - Conjuration
- **School:** Conjuration
- **Tier:** 2

## Base Costs
- **Mana:** 15 MP (one-time summoning)
- **Stamina:** 0 SP
- **Range:** 10 feet
- **Duration:** Permanent (until familiar dies or is dismissed)
- **Cooldown:** Can only have one familiar at a time

## Grimoire Casting Costs
**As Ritual (1 hour):**
- Mana: **15 MP** (can be 0 MP with [[Ritual Specialist]])
- Stamina: **0 SP**
- Components: 10 gold worth of incense and herbs (consumed)
- Must have grimoire open during ritual

## Effect
You summon a fey spirit that takes the form of a small beast. Choose its form when summoning.

### Familiar Forms
Choose one (cosmetic choice, stats are the same):
- Cat, Raven, Owl, Bat, Rat, Snake, Spider, Toad, Weasel, Crab, Fish, Lizard, Octopus

### Familiar Stats
- **HP:** 5 + (Arcana/2)
- **AC:** 12 + (Arcana/4)
- **Speed:** 30 feet (or fly/swim depending on form)
- **Abilities:** Darkvision 60ft, can't attack, can deliver touch spells

### Familiar Capabilities
1. **Senses:** You can see and hear through your familiar (up to 100 feet away)
2. **Telepathy:** Communicate telepathically while within 100 feet
3. **Touch Spell Delivery:** Familiar can deliver touch-range spells
4. **Scout:** Perfect for reconnaissance
5. **Help Action:** Can use Help action in combat to give you advantage

## Scaling
- At Arcana 18+: Familiar gains your proficiency bonus to all checks
- At Arcana 24+: Range increases to 1 mile, familiar can speak one language
- At Arcana 30+: Familiar can turn invisible at will
- At Arcana 36+: Familiar can attack (1d4 damage)
- At Arcana 42+: You have two familiars simultaneously

## Special
**If Familiar Dies:**
- Disappears in puff of smoke, returns to spirit realm
- You can resummon after 1 day
- Same cost to resummon
- Can choose different form

**Familiar Bond:**
- Familiar is loyal and intelligent (can follow complex orders)
- Shares your alignment
- Cannot be charmed or controlled by others
- If you die, familiar disappears

## Tactical Uses
- **Scouting:** Send familiar ahead to scout dangerous areas
- **Spell Delivery:** Cast touch spells from range via familiar
- **Distraction:** Familiar can create diversions
- **Detection:** Use familiar's senses to detect traps, enemies, treasure
- **Roleplay:** Wizard with owl familiar is iconic

### Spell Delivery Examples
- Cast [[Shocking Grasp]] through familiar (touch at range)
- Cast healing touch spells through familiar
- Deliver buff spells to distant allies

## Inscription
- **Time:** 4 hours (Tier 2)
- **Cost:** 100 gold
- **Source:** Common in conjuration grimoires, popular starting spell
