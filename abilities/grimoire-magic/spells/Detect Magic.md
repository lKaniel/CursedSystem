# Detect Magic

## Description
A fundamental divination ritual that reveals the presence and nature of magical auras around you.

## Prerequisites (to inscribe)
- **Intellect:** 8
- **Arcana:** 8

## Details
- **Type:** Grimoire Spell (Divination Ritual)
- **Category:** [[Grimoire Magic]] - Divination
- **School:** Divination
- **Tier:** 1

## Base Costs
- **Mana:** 6 MP
- **Stamina:** 2 SP
- **Range:** 30-foot radius (self)
- **Duration:** 10 minutes (concentration)
- **Cooldown:** None

## Grimoire Casting Costs
**Without Mastery Abilities:**
- Mana: 6 MP Ã— 1.5 = **9 MP**
- Stamina: 2 + 3 = **5 SP**
- Casting time: 1 minute of ritual reading
- Must have grimoire open during entire duration

**With [[Rapid Reference]] + [[Ritual Specialist]]:**
- Mana: **6 MP** (or 0 MP if cast as 10-minute ritual)
- Stamina: **2 SP**
- Casting time: 1 action (or 10 minutes for free)
- Grimoire just needs to be nearby

## Effect
For the duration, you sense the presence of magic within 30 feet of you.

**Information Revealed:**
1. **Presence:** Whether magic is present
2. **Location:** General direction and distance to magical auras
3. **Intensity:** Strength of the magic (faint, moderate, strong, overwhelming)
4. **School:** Type of magic (evocation, abjuration, etc.) with successful Arcana check

**Arcana Check DC:**
- DC 12: Identify school of magic
- DC 16: Identify specific spell effect
- DC 20: Identify exact spell and caster's approximate power level

## Scaling
- At Arcana 16+: Range increases to 60 feet
- At Arcana 24+: Can see through walls and barriers
- At Arcana 32+: Can identify magical traps and wards
- At Arcana 40+: Can sense magic up to 120 feet, identify caster if they're nearby

## Ritual Casting
Can be cast as a 10-minute ritual for **0 MP cost** (if you have [[Ritual Specialist]]).

## Inscription
- **Time:** 2 hours (Tier 1)
- **Cost:** 50 gold
- **Source:** Found in nearly all grimoires, very common scroll

## Special
- Essential utility spell for dungeon delving
- Can identify cursed items before touching them
- Detect invisible magical creatures
- See magical auras through illusions
- Does not detect creatures with innate magic (like dragons) unless they're actively using magic
