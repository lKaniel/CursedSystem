# Arcane Bolt

## Description
A fundamental evocation spell found in nearly every grimoire, firing a concentrated beam of pure arcane energy.

## Prerequisites (to inscribe)
- **Intellect:** 8
- **Arcana:** 6

## Details
- **Type:** Grimoire Spell (Evocation)
- **Category:** [[Grimoire Magic]] - Evocation
- **School:** Evocation
- **Tier:** 1

## Base Costs (before grimoire penalties)
- **Mana:** 5 MP
- **Stamina:** 2 SP
- **Range:** 120 feet
- **Cooldown:** None

## Grimoire Casting Costs
**Without Mastery Abilities:**
- Mana: 5 MP Ã— 1.5 = **7-8 MP**
- Stamina: 2 + 3 = **5 SP**
- Reference: 3 SP (1 action)
- Must be stationary, grimoire in hand
- Spell effect delayed to next turn

**With [[Rapid Reference]] + [[Mana Efficiency]] + [[Effortless Casting]]:**
- Mana: **5 MP** (base)
- Stamina: **2 SP** (base)
- Reference: 0 SP (free)
- Can cast while moving
- Instant effect

## Effect
Make a ranged spell attack. On hit, deal force damage.

**Damage Scaling:** 1d10 + (Intellect/2) base, +1d10 per 8 Arcana
- Arcana 6: 1d10 + (Int/2) damage
- Arcana 14: 2d10 + (Int/2) damage
- Arcana 22: 3d10 + (Int/2) damage
- Arcana 30: 4d10 + (Int/2) damage

**Attack Roll:** d(Intellect/2 + Arcana/2) + (Intellect/2 + Arcana/2) vs target's DC

## Inscription
- **Time:** 2 hours (Tier 1)
- **Cost:** 50 gold
- **Source:** Common in most grimoires, basic spell scroll

## Special
- Force damage (rarely resisted)
- Can target objects to break them
- Leaves no mark except pure energy impact
- Popular first spell for grimoire wizards
