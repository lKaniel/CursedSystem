# Living Grimoire

## Description
The ultimate grimoire mastery - your body becomes a living repository of arcane knowledge, spells inscribed upon your very soul.

## Prerequisites
- **Intellect:** 22
- **Arcana:** 40
- **Endurance:** 16

## Details
- **Type:** Passive Ability (Transformation)
- **Category:** [[Grimoire Magic]] - Legendary
- **Cost:** 1 Ability Point
- **Cooldown:** N/A (Permanent)

## Effect
Your primary grimoire merges with your essence, becoming a part of you.

### Benefits
1. **Cannot Be Disarmed:** Your grimoire cannot be stolen, lost, or destroyed
2. **Always Available:** Can cast grimoire spells even if physically restrained
3. **Instant Access:** All spells are mentally available, no referencing needed
4. **Resilient:** Grimoire exists in multiple planes, backed up across reality
5. **Manifest/Dismiss:** Can manifest grimoire as physical book at will (free action)
6. **Soul-Bound:** If you die, grimoire persists and can be recovered

### Physical Manifestation
When manifested as a physical book:
- Can let others read it
- Can inscribe new spells into it
- Appears as ornate tome covered in glowing runes
- Indestructible to physical damage

### Spiritual Form
When in soul-bound form:
- Spells appear as mental images
- Can "flip pages" mentally
- New spells inscribed through meditation
- Visible as glowing arcane tattoos on your skin (optional cosmetic)

## Scaling
- At Arcana 48+: Can have 2 living grimoires simultaneously
- At Arcana 56+: Your living grimoire grants +2 to Arcane spell save DC
- At Arcana 64+: Casting from living grimoire costs -20% mana

## Special
- This is one of the pinnacle grimoire abilities
- Makes you nearly impossible to "de-power" by removing items
- Your grimoire becomes part of your legacy
- If resurrected, grimoire returns with you

### Synergies
- **Living Grimoire + Grimoire Master:** Multiple grimoires, one living (unloseable)
- **Living Grimoire + Spell Fusion:** Fuse spells mentally without referencing
- **Living Grimoire + Battle Caster:** True battle mage, zero restrictions
