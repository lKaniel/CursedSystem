# Battle Caster

## Description
Master the art of combat spellcasting, weaving magic even while engaged in melee and under pressure.

## Prerequisites
- **Intellect:** 14
- **Arcana:** 14
- **Agility:** 10

## Details
- **Type:** Passive Ability
- **Category:** [[Grimoire Magic]] - Core Mastery
- **Cost:** 1 Ability Point
- **Cooldown:** N/A (Permanent)

## Effect
You can cast grimoire spells in combat situations that would normally prevent casting.

### Benefits
1. **Combat Casting:** Can cast grimoire spells while in melee range of enemies
2. **Mobile Casting:** Can cast while moving at full speed (including sprinting)
3. **Defensive Casting:** Being hit doesn't interrupt your casting
4. **Concentration Protection:** Advantage on saves to maintain concentration when damaged

### No Longer Restricted By
- Enemies in melee range
- Movement
- Opportunity attacks
- Taking damage

## Scaling
- At Arcana 20+: Can cast grimoire spells while grappled
- At Arcana 28+: Can cast while prone or restrained
- At Arcana 35+: Can cast while falling or underwater

## Special
- Essential for "gish" (warrior-mage) builds
- Allows full mobility in combat
- Synergizes with melee/magic hybrid characters
- Still requires grimoire to be accessible (within 5 feet)

## Tactics
- Cast buff spells while charging into melee
- Cast debuffs while kiting enemies
- Maintain mobility while controlling the battlefield

- **Requires:** [[Rapid Reference]]
- **Unlocks:** [[Spell Modification]], [[Grimoire Master]]
- **Synergizes with:** [[Light Melee]], [[Acrobatics]], mobility builds
