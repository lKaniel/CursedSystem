# Arcane Grimoire

## Description
Acquire your first arcane grimoire, a mystical tome that allows you to inscribe and cast spells through scholarly study and preparation.

## Prerequisites
- **Intellect:** 10
- **Arcana:** 8

## Details
- **Type:** Passive Ability
- **Category:** [[Grimoire Magic]] - Core
- **Cost:** 1 Ability Point
- **Cooldown:** N/A (Permanent)

## Effect
You gain your first **Arcane Grimoire**, a physical spellbook that can hold unlimited spells.

### Grimoire Capabilities
- **Spell Capacity:** Unlimited
- **Inscription:** Can inscribe any spell you find (scrolls, other grimoires, research)
- **Inscription Time:** 2 hours per spell tier
- **Inscription Cost:** 50 gold per spell tier
- **Starting Spells:** 4 basic spells of your choice (tier 1-2)

### Casting from Grimoire
When you cast a spell from your grimoire:
- **Mana Cost:** Base spell cost + 50%
- **Stamina Cost:** Base spell SP + 3 SP
- **Preparation:** Must use 1 action (3 SP) to reference grimoire before casting
- **Requirements:** Must have grimoire in hand or within 5 feet, must be stationary, one hand free
- **Casting Time:** Spell takes effect at the start of your next turn (1 round delay)

### Grimoire Properties
- **HP:** 10 (can be damaged)
- **Weight:** 3 lbs
- **Size:** Medium book
- **Bonded:** Only you can read your grimoire's spells
- **Recovery:** If lost, you can recreate spells from memory (costs normal inscription time/gold)

## Scaling
- At Arcana 15+: Grimoire can hold magical properties (becomes magical item)
- At Arcana 22+: Can attune grimoire to respond to mental commands (no need to hold it)
- At Arcana 30+: Grimoire becomes indestructible (can still be stolen)

## Special
- You can own multiple grimoires, but can only use one at a time without [[Grimoire Master]]
- Other wizards cannot read or cast from your grimoire without your permission
- If grimoire is destroyed, you lose access to all inscribed spells until recreated

## Progression
- **Unlocks:** [[Grimoire Apprentice]], [[Efficient Inscription]]
- **Required for:** All other Grimoire Magic abilities
