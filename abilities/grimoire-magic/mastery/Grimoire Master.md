# Grimoire Master

## Description
Transcend the limitations of a single tome, wielding multiple grimoires simultaneously and switching between them with a thought.

## Prerequisites
- **Intellect:** 18
- **Arcana:** 26
- **Awareness:** 12

## Details
- **Type:** Passive Ability
- **Category:** [[Grimoire Magic]] - Master
- **Cost:** 1 Ability Point
- **Cooldown:** N/A (Permanent)

## Effect
You can attune to and use multiple grimoires simultaneously.

### Benefits
1. **Multiple Grimoires:** Attune to up to 3 grimoires at once
2. **Instant Switching:** Switch active grimoire as a free action (0 SP)
3. **Shared Access:** Can cast from any attuned grimoire within 30 feet
4. **Mental Link:** Don't need to physically hold or see grimoires to cast
5. **Grimoire Sense:** Always know location and status of attuned grimoires

### Tactical Uses
- **Specialized Grimoires:** One for combat, one for utility, one for rituals
- **Element Grimoires:** Fire spells, ice spells, lightning spells
- **School Grimoires:** Evocation, abjuration, divination, etc.
- **Backup Grimoires:** If one is stolen/destroyed, you have others
- **Spell Organization:** Group spells by theme for quick access

## Scaling
- At Arcana 32+: Can attune to 4 grimoires
- At Arcana 38+: Can attune to 5 grimoires
- At Arcana 44+: Can attune to 6 grimoires
- At Arcana 50+: Can attune to unlimited grimoires

## Special
- Each grimoire can have different properties/enchantments
- Can share grimoires with allies (they can read but not cast unless they have grimoire abilities)
- Lost grimoires remain attuned until you break the bond
- Can sense if someone is tampering with your attuned grimoires

### Example Build: The Librarian
- **Combat Grimoire:** 30 offensive spells (fire, lightning, force)
- **Defense Grimoire:** 25 protective spells (wards, barriers, dispels)
- **Utility Grimoire:** 40 utility spells (teleport, scrying, enchantments)
- **Total:** 95+ spells accessible instantly
