# Effortless Casting

## Description
Grimoire spells flow from your lips as naturally as breathing, requiring no additional stamina beyond the spell's base cost.

## Prerequisites
- **Intellect:** 16
- **Arcana:** 20
- **Endurance:** 8

## Details
- **Type:** Passive Ability
- **Category:** [[Grimoire Magic]] - Cost Reduction
- **Cost:** 1 Ability Point
- **Cooldown:** N/A (Permanent)

## Effect
Remove the +3 SP penalty from grimoire casting. Your grimoire spells now cost **base SP** only.

### Before vs After
**Before Effortless Casting:**
- Spell costs 3 SP base
- Grimoire casting: 3 + 3 = **6 SP**

**After Effortless Casting:**
- Spell costs 3 SP base
- Grimoire casting: **3 SP** (same as innate casting)

### Combined with Other Abilities
**With [[Rapid Reference]] + [[Effortless Casting]]:**
- Reference: 0 SP (free at Arcana 18+)
- Casting: Base SP only
- **Total overhead: 0 SP!**

## Scaling
- At Arcana 28+: Grimoire spells cost -1 SP (minimum 0 SP)
- At Arcana 36+: Grimoire spells cost -2 SP (minimum 0 SP)
- At Arcana 44+: Cantrips from grimoire are completely free (0 MP, 0 SP)

## Special
- Combined with [[Mana Efficiency]], grimoire casting has no penalties
- At high levels, grimoire casting becomes more efficient than innate
- This is the "payoff" for investing in grimoire mastery

## Power Spike
**With Mana Efficiency + Effortless Casting + Rapid Reference:**
- No mana penalty
- No SP penalty
- No delay
- No movement restriction
- Can cast while in melee
- **Grimoire = 80+ spells with zero penalties**

## Progression
- **Requires:** [[Mana Efficiency]], [[Battle Caster]]
- **Unlocks:** [[Metamagic Mastery]], [[Spell Fusion]]
- **Synergizes with:** All grimoire abilities
