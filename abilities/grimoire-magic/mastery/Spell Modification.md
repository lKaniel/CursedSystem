# Spell Modification

## Description
Your deep understanding of arcane formulae allows you to alter spell effects on the fly, customizing each casting to the situation.

## Prerequisites
- **Intellect:** 16
- **Arcana:** 22
- **Crafting:** 10

## Details
- **Type:** Active Ability
- **Category:** [[Grimoire Magic]] - Expert Mastery
- **Cost:** Variable (see below)
- **Cooldown:** Per spell cast

## Effect
When casting a spell from your grimoire, you can modify it by spending additional resources.

### Available Modifications

**Extended Range** (Cost: +3 MP)
- Double the spell's range
- Example: 60 feet → 120 feet

**Extended Duration** (Cost: +5 MP)
- Double the spell's duration
- Doesn't work on instantaneous spells

**Additional Target** (Cost: +4 MP per target)
- Add one additional target to single-target spells
- Can stack multiple times

**Increased Area** (Cost: +6 MP)
- Increase AoE radius by 50%
- Example: 20-foot radius → 30-foot radius

**Empowered** (Cost: +8 MP)
- Add +(Arcana/4) to one damage or healing die per die rolled
- Example: 5d6 becomes 5d6+5 at Arcana 20

**Quickened** (Cost: +4 SP)
- Cast as reaction instead of action
- Can only quicken spells that normally cost 1 action

**Subtle** (Cost: +2 SP)
- Cast without verbal or somatic components
- Cannot be countered or noticed

**Transmuted** (Cost: +5 MP)
- Change damage type (fire → cold → lightning → acid → poison → thunder)

## Scaling
- At Arcana 28+: Modification costs reduced by 25%
- At Arcana 35+: Can apply two modifications to the same spell
- At Arcana 42+: Can apply three modifications to the same spell

## Special
- Modifications decided when casting (not when inscribing)
- Different modifications can stack
- Some modification combinations are synergistic
- Can't modify spells beyond their fundamental nature

### Modification Combos
**Empowered + Extended Range + Quickened:**
- Powerful long-range reaction spell
- Great for countering distant threats

**Subtle + Additional Targets + Transmuted:**
- Hidden multi-target spell with custom damage type
- Perfect for ambushes
