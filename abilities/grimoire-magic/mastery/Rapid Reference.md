# Rapid Reference

## Description
Your familiarity with your grimoire allows you to quickly flip to the right page and cast spells without delay.

## Prerequisites
- **Intellect:** 12
- **Arcana:** 12

## Details
- **Type:** Passive Ability
- **Category:** [[Grimoire Magic]] - Core Mastery
- **Cost:** 1 Ability Point
- **Cooldown:** N/A (Permanent)

## Effect
Your grimoire casting becomes significantly faster and more fluid.

### Benefits
1. **No Casting Delay:** Spells from grimoire take effect immediately (no 1 round delay)
2. **Quick Reference:** Referencing grimoire now costs only 1 SP (down from 3 SP action)
3. **Reference While Moving:** You can reference and cast grimoire spells while moving
4. **One-Handed Casting:** Can hold grimoire with one hand and cast with the same hand

### Casting Process (Updated)
- **Before:** Stop, reference (3 SP action), cast spell (+50% MP, +3 SP), wait 1 round
- **After:** Reference (1 SP), cast spell immediately (+50% MP, +3 SP), can move normally

## Scaling
- At Arcana 18+: Can reference grimoire as part of casting (no separate SP cost)
- At Arcana 25+: Can cast while running or performing acrobatics

## Special
- This makes grimoire casting combat-viable
- Significantly reduces the action economy burden
- Enables "battle wizard" playstyle

## Progression
- **Requires:** [[Arcane Grimoire]]
- **Unlocks:** [[Battle Caster]], [[Prepared Caster]]
- **Synergizes with:** [[Mana Efficiency]], [[Effortless Casting]]
