# Swords

Blade mastery and sword techniques.

## Overview

Sword abilities work with various blade types, from longswords to rapiers to katanas.

**Stat Requirements:** *Might OR Agility (depending on sword type)*

## Weapon Types

### Heavy Swords (Might)
- Longsword
- Greatsword
- Bastard Sword
- Claymore

### Light Swords (Agility)
- Rapier
- Scimitar
- Katana
- Shortsword

## Skill Progression

### Tier 0: Proficiency
**[[Sword Proficiency]]**
- Prerequisites: Might 4 OR Agility 4
- Basic competence with all swords

### Tier 1: Basic Techniques
**[[Blade Mastery]]**
- Prerequisites: [[Sword Proficiency]], Might 8 OR Agility 8, Relevant Melee 6
- +2 to hit, bonus damage, improved crits

### Tier 2: Advanced Techniques
**[[Riposte]]**
- Prerequisites: [[Blade Mastery]], Agility 10, Light Melee 8
- Counter-attack after enemy misses

**[[Whirling Blade]]**
- Prerequisites: [[Riposte]], Agility 14 OR Might 14, Melee 12
- Spin attack hits all adjacent enemies

### Tier 3: Expert Techniques
**[[Sword Dance]]**
- Prerequisites: [[Whirling Blade]], Agility 16, Light Melee 14, Acrobatics 10
- Reduce action costs, graceful fighting style

### Tier 4: Master Techniques
**[[Iaijutsu Strike]]**
- Prerequisites: [[Sword Dance]], Agility 18, Light Melee 16
- Lightning draw-and-strike, massive damage

**[[Perfect Cut]]**
- Prerequisites: [[Iaijutsu Strike]], Agility 20, Light Melee 20
- Ignore armor, expanded crit range, auto-crit option
