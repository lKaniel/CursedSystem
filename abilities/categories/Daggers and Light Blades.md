# Daggers & Light Blades

Swift strikes and precision combat.

## Overview

Fast, precise weapons perfect for quick attacks and sneaky tactics.

**Stat Requirements:** *Agility + Light Melee*

## Weapon Types

- Dagger
- Stiletto
- Kukri
- Throwing Knife
- Parrying Dagger

## Skill Progression

### Tier 0: Proficiency
**[[Dagger Proficiency]]**
- Prerequisites: Agility 4
- Use daggers and light blades effectively

### Tier 1: Basic Techniques
**[[Dual Wielding]]**
- Prerequisites: [[Dagger Proficiency]], Agility 8, Light Melee 6
- Fight with two weapons, reduced off-hand cost

**[[Quick Strike]]**
- Prerequisites: [[Dual Wielding]], Agility 12, Light Melee 10
- Lightning-fast attack for 2 SP

### Tier 2: Advanced Techniques
**[[Backstab]]**
- Prerequisites: [[Quick Strike]], Agility 14, Light Melee 12, Stealth 8
- Massive bonus damage from behind/stealth

### Tier 3: Expert Techniques
**[[Shadow Knife]]**
- Prerequisites: [[Backstab]], Agility 16, Light Melee 14, Stealth 12
- Teleport behind enemy and strike

**[[Flurry of Daggers]]**
- Prerequisites: [[Shadow Knife]] OR [[Dual Wielding]] + Agility 16, Agility 18, Light Melee 18
- 6 rapid attacks in succession

### Tier 4: Master Techniques
**[[Vital Strike]]**
- Prerequisites: [[Flurry of Daggers]], Agility 20, Light Melee 20, Stealth 16
- Precision strike with potential instant kill
