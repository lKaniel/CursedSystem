# Polearms & Halberds

Reach weapons and formation fighting.

## Overview

Long weapons that allow fighting from distance, controlling space, and formation tactics.

**Stat Requirements:** *Might + Heavy Melee*

## Weapon Types

- Spear
- Pike
- Halberd
- Glaive
- Quarterstaff
- Trident

## Skill Progression

### Tier 0: Proficiency
**[[Polearm Proficiency]]**
- Prerequisites: Might 6
- Use reach weapons effectively

### Tier 1: Basic Techniques
**[[Reach Advantage]]**
- Prerequisites: [[Polearm Proficiency]], Might 8, Heavy Melee 6
- Control space, opportunity attacks when enemies enter reach

### Tier 2: Advanced Techniques
**[[Sweeping Strike]]**
- Prerequisites: [[Reach Advantage]], Might 12, Heavy Melee 10
- Wide arc hitting enemies in a line

**[[Impale]]**
- Prerequisites: [[Sweeping Strike]], Might 14, Heavy Melee 12
- Pin enemy, ongoing damage, restrained

### Tier 3: Expert Techniques
**[[Pike Wall]]**
- Prerequisites: [[Impale]], Might 18, Heavy Melee 16
- Create defensive zone enemies cannot cross

### Tier 4: Master Techniques
**[[Skewer]]**
- Prerequisites: [[Pike Wall]], Might 20, Heavy Melee 20
- Pierce through multiple enemies in a line
