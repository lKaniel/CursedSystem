# Thrown Weapons

Hurling weapons with precision and power.

## Overview

Weapons designed to be thrown, requiring both strength and accuracy.

**Stat Requirements:** *Agility OR Might + Ranged Combat*

## Weapon Types

- Throwing Axe
- Throwing Dagger/Knife
- Javelin
- Chakram
- Boomerang
- Shuriken

## Skill Progression

### Tier 0: Proficiency
**[[Thrown Weapon Proficiency]]**
- Prerequisites: Agility 4 OR Might 4
- Throw weapons effectively

### Tier 1: Basic Techniques
**[[Quick Toss]]**
- Prerequisites: [[Thrown Weapon Proficiency]], Agility/Might 8, Ranged Combat 6
- Fast throw for reduced cost

### Tier 2: Advanced Techniques
**[[Returning Weapon]]**
- Prerequisites: [[Quick Toss]], Agility 10, Ranged Combat 8, Arcana 6
- Weapons magically return to hand

**[[Multi-Throw]]**
- Prerequisites: [[Quick Toss]], Agility/Might 14, Ranged Combat 12
- Throw 4 weapons simultaneously

### Tier 3: Expert Techniques
**[[Boomerang Strike]]**
- Prerequisites: [[Returning Weapon]], Agility 16, Ranged Combat 16
- Weapon hits twice in an arc

### Tier 4: Master Techniques
**[[Perfect Aim]]**
- Prerequisites: [[Multi-Throw]] OR [[Boomerang Strike]], Agility/Might 20, Ranged Combat 20
- Never miss, expanded crit range
