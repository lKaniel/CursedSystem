# Endurance Abilities

Toughness, stamina, and physical resilience.

## Overview

Abilities that enhance your durability, stamina recovery, and resistance to harm.

**Stat Requirements:** *Endurance + Fortitude/Stamina*

## Ability Categories

### Recovery
- **Second Wind** - Heal yourself mid-combat
- **Fast Healing** - Regenerate HP over time
- **Tireless** - Reduced stamina costs

### Toughness
- **Iron Will** - Resist mental effects
- **Tough as Nails** - Bonus HP
- **Damage Reduction** - Reduce all incoming damage

### Stamina Management
- **Stamina Surge** - Burst of extra stamina
- **Efficient Movement** - Move for less stamina
- **Battle Rhythm** - Recover more stamina per turn

### Resilience
- **Relentless** - Keep fighting at 0 HP
- **Regeneration** - Automatic healing each turn
- **Unstoppable** - Immune to certain conditions

### Master Techniques
- **Last Stand** - Massive power when near death
- **Phoenix Heart** - Revive yourself once per day
- **Titan's Endurance** - Temporary invulnerability
