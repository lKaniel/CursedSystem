# Armor & Shields

Defensive equipment mastery and protection.

## Overview

Abilities that enhance your use of armor and shields for superior defense.

**Stat Requirements:** *Endurance OR Agility (depending on armor type)*

## Armor Types

### Light Armor (Agility)
- Padded
- Leather
- Studded Leather

### Medium Armor (Endurance or Agility)
- Hide
- Chain Shirt
- Scale Mail

### Heavy Armor (Endurance)
- Ring Mail
- Chain Mail
- Plate Armor

## Shield Types

- Buckler (Light)
- Round Shield
- Kite Shield
- Tower Shield (Heavy)

## Abilities

### Armor Mastery
- **Light Armor Mastery** - No movement penalty, bonus AC
- **Medium Armor Training** - Better AC from medium armor
- **Heavy Armor Proficiency** - Wear heavy armor effectively
- **Armor Specialization** - Enhanced benefits from chosen armor type

### Shield Techniques
- **Shield Master** - Use shield offensively and defensively
- **Shield Bash** - Attack with shield
- **Shield Wall** - Formation defense with allies
- **Tower Shield Defense** - Total cover with tower shield

### Advanced Techniques
- **Deflection** - Redirect attacks
- **Fortress Stance** - Immobile but extreme defense
- **Armor Adaptation** - Reduce armor check penalties
