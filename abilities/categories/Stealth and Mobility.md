# Stealth & Mobility

Shadow arts, sneaking, and acrobatic movement.

## Overview

Abilities for moving unseen, striking from shadows, and acrobatic maneuvers.

**Stat Requirements:** *Agility + Stealth/Acrobatics*

## Ability Categories

### Stealth
- **Hide in Shadows** - Become hidden in dim light
- **Silent Movement** - Move without sound
- **Camouflage** - Blend into environment

### Assassination
- **Sneak Attack** - Extra damage from stealth
- **Assassinate** - Critical hit on surprised targets
- **Death Strike** - Instant kill from stealth

### Mobility
- **Evasion** - Dodge area effects completely
- **Acrobatic Dodge** - Tumble through danger
- **Wall Running** - Run along vertical surfaces

### Advanced Stealth
- **Invisibility** - Magical concealment
- **Shadow Step** - Teleport through shadows
- **Master of Disguise** - Perfect impersonation

### Master Techniques
- **Perfect Stealth** - Invisible even when moving
- **Vanish** - Disappear mid-combat
- **Shadow Clone** - Create illusory doubles
