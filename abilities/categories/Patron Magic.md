# Patron Magic (Cleric/Warlock)

Divine/Pact spellcasting - magic granted through a patron.

## Overview

Patron Magic represents power granted by an external entity: a deity, demon, fey lord, or other powerful being. You cast **through** your patron.

**Stat Requirements:** *Awareness + Insight OR Presence*

## How It Works

- Power comes from your **Patron** (deity, demon, archfey, etc.)
- Must maintain relationship with patron
- Patron may grant or withhold powers
- Breaking pact can have severe consequences

## Patron Types

### Divine Patrons (Clerics)
- Gods and goddesses
- Grants healing, protection, holy magic
- Requires faith and worship
- Stats: Awareness + Insight

### Pact Patrons (Warlocks)
- Demons, devils, archfey, old ones
- Grants eldritch powers
- Requires service or payment
- Stats: Presence (force of will)

## Example Spells

### Divine (Cleric)
- Cure Wounds
- Bless
- Turn Undead
- Divine Shield
- Resurrection
- Holy Smite

### Pact (Warlock)
- Eldritch Blast
- Hex
- Armor of Agathys
- Hunger of Hadar
- Finger of Death
- Power Word Kill

## Patron Relationships

Your patron may:
- Grant bonus abilities for faithful service
- Demand quests or sacrifices
- Withdraw powers if displeased
- Provide guidance or visions

## Prerequisites

_Varies by patron and spell_

## Scaling

Divine spells scale with Awareness + Insight
Pact spells scale with Presence
