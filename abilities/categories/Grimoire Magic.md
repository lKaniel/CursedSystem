# Grimoire Magic

Scholarly spellcasting through arcane grimoires - vast spell libraries at the cost of preparation and restrictions.

## Overview

Grimoire Magic is a **tome-based** magic system where you acquire and master mystical grimoires. Unlike [[Innate Magic]] where you know spells instinctively, grimoires allow you to inscribe and cast **unlimited spells**, but with significant drawbacks that diminish as you master the grimoire arts.

**Core Concept:** Trade flexibility and spell variety for increased costs and casting restrictions.

## How Grimoire Magic Works

### The Grimoire
- A **physical artifact** containing inscribed spells
- Can hold **unlimited spells** (no spell known limit)
- Must be **in hand or nearby** to cast from it
- Can be lost, stolen, or destroyed
- Takes time to inscribe new spells

### Casting from Grimoire

**Base Restrictions (without mastery abilities):**
- Costs **+50% Mana** and **+3 SP** more than the spell's base cost
- Requires **1 action (3 SP)** to reference the grimoire before casting
- Must have **at least one hand free** to hold/reference the book
- Cannot cast while **moving** (must be stationary)
- Spell takes **1 round longer** to cast (delayed to next turn)
- Some spells require **material components** or **ritual conditions**

**Why Use Grimoires Then?**
- Learn **any spell** you find (scrolls, other grimoires, research)
- No ability point cost to learn spells (only to acquire grimoire mastery)
- Can have **hundreds of spells** available
- Abilities in the tree **remove restrictions** and **reduce costs**
- Eventually becomes **more powerful** than innate casting

### Learning Spells

**Once you have a grimoire, you can inscribe spells by:**
- Finding spell scrolls (common treasure)
- Copying from other grimoires (with permission or... acquisition)
- Researching in libraries (costs time + gold)
- Quest rewards and discovered arcane texts
- **No ability point cost** - just time and resources

**Inscription Cost:**
- Time: 2 hours per spell tier
- Gold: 50g Ã— spell tier
- Must meet spell's Intellect/Arcana prerequisites to inscribe

## Grimoire Mastery Progression

The ability tree focuses on **improving grimoire casting** and **removing restrictions**.

### Core Path: Grimoire Acquisition & Basics
1. [[Arcane Grimoire]] - Acquire your first grimoire, learn basic inscription
2. [[Grimoire Apprentice]] - Reduce casting costs, faster inscription
3. [[Rapid Reference]] - Cast without delay, reference while moving
4. [[Battle Caster]] - Cast grimoire spells while moving and in melee
5. [[Grimoire Master]] - Multiple grimoires, instant switching

### Advanced Path: Cost Reduction
1. [[Efficient Inscription]] - Reduced inscription time and gold cost
2. [[Mana Efficiency]] - Grimoire spells cost normal mana (remove +50% penalty)
3. [[Effortless Casting]] - Remove extra SP cost from grimoire casting
4. [[Overcharged Casting]] - Spend extra mana to boost spell effects

### Expert Path: Ritual & Preparation
1. [[Ritual Specialist]] - Cast grimoire spells as powerful rituals
2. [[Prepared Caster]] - Pre-load spells for instant casting
3. [[Spell Modification]] - Alter spell effects on the fly
4. [[Metamagic Mastery]] - Modify range, duration, targets, damage type

### Master Path: Multiple Grimoires
1. [[Secondary Grimoire]] - Attune to a second grimoire
2. [[Grimoire Library]] - Manage 3+ grimoires, quick switching
3. [[Spell Fusion]] - Combine spells from different grimoires
4. [[Living Grimoire]] - Your body becomes a grimoire, can't be disarmed

## Key Differences: Grimoire vs Innate

| Aspect | Innate Magic | Grimoire Magic |
|--------|-------------|----------------|
| **Spell Limit** | Limited by ability points | Unlimited (inscribe any found spell) |
| **Learning Cost** | 1 ability point per spell | Time + gold only |
| **Casting Cost** | Base cost | +50% MP, +3 SP (initially) |
| **Casting Speed** | Instant | Delayed (initially) |
| **Requirements** | Just stats | Must have grimoire, stationary, free hand (initially) |
| **Flexibility** | Fixed spells known | Huge spell library |
| **Power Ceiling** | Moderate | Very high (with mastery) |
| **Risk** | None | Grimoire can be lost/stolen |

## Mastery Progression Example

**Level 1 Grimoire Wizard:**
- Has [[Arcane Grimoire]] ability
- Grimoire contains 6 spells (found 3 scrolls, inscribed 3 researched spells)
- Must stop moving, hold grimoire, costs +50% MP and +3 SP, spell delays 1 round
- **Clunky but versatile**

**Level 10 Grimoire Wizard:**
- Has [[Rapid Reference]], [[Mana Efficiency]], [[Battle Caster]]
- Grimoire contains 30+ spells
- Can cast while moving, no delay, normal mana cost, only +3 SP
- **Much smoother, huge spell variety**

**Level 20 Grimoire Wizard:**
- Has [[Effortless Casting]], [[Grimoire Master]], [[Spell Modification]]
- Two grimoires with 80+ spells total
- Can cast normally with no penalties, can modify spells mid-cast
- **Extremely versatile and powerful**
