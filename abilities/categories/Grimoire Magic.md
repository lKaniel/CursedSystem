# Grimoire Magic (Wizard)

Scholarly spellcasting - magic inscribed in your spellbook.

## Overview

Grimoire Magic represents spells **INSCRIBED** in a spellbook. You learn by studying and writing, with harder prerequisites but greater versatility.

**Stat Requirements:** *Intellect + Arcana*

## How It Works

- Spells must be **written in your spellbook** to cast
- Requires owning and maintaining a spellbook
- **Harder prerequisites** than Innate Magic
- Can learn spells from scrolls, other spellbooks, or research
- If spellbook is lost, must recreate from memory

## Spellbook Mechanics

### Acquiring Spells
- Learn through leveling (free spells)
- Copy from scrolls (costs gold + time)
- Copy from other spellbooks (costs gold + time)
- Research new spells (expensive, time-consuming)

### Spellbook Details
- Capacity: Unlimited
- Cost to copy spell: 50 gold per spell level + 2 hours
- Backup spellbooks recommended

## Example Spells

### Utility & Detection

### Combat & Control

- Dispel Magic

### High-Level Magic

- Clone

## Prerequisites

_Higher than Innate Magic - requires more study and preparation_

## Scaling

All grimoire spells scale with Intellect and Arcana.
