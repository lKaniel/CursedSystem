# Utility & Passives

Non-combat abilities and always-active bonuses.

## Overview

Abilities that provide utility, quality of life improvements, and passive benefits.

**Stat Requirements:** *Varies by ability*

## Ability Categories

### Vision & Senses
- **Darkvision** - See in darkness
- **Blindsight** - Sense without seeing
- **Tremorsense** - Detect vibrations

### Magic Utility
- **Arcane Recovery** - Regain mana on short rest
- **Ritual Casting** - Cast spells without mana (slower)
- **Spell Storing** - Store spells for later use

### Skills & Tools
- **Tool Expertise** - Master craftsman abilities
- **Jack of All Trades** - Bonus to all skills
- **Expertise** - Double proficiency in chosen skills

### Movement
- **Fast Movement** - Increased base speed
- **Spider Climb** - Walk on walls/ceilings
- **Water Walking** - Walk on liquid surfaces

### Defense
- **Unarmored Defense** - AC bonus without armor
- **Magic Resistance** - Advantage vs spells
- **Damage Resistance** - Resist specific damage type

### Survival
- **Forager** - Always find food
- **Tracker** - Enhanced tracking abilities
- **Weather Sense** - Predict weather

### Language & Communication
- **Polyglot** - Learn languages easily
- **Telepathy** - Mental communication
- **Speak with Animals** - Talk to beasts
