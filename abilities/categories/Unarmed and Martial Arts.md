# Unarmed & Martial Arts

Fighting without weapons using body as the weapon.

## Overview

Martial arts and unarmed combat techniques, from boxing to monk-style fighting.

**Stat Requirements:** *Might OR Agility*

## Fighting Styles

### Power-Based (Might)
- Boxing
- Wrestling
- Sumo

### Speed-Based (Agility)
- Kung Fu
- Karate
- Capoeira

## Abilities

### Basic Techniques
- **Stunning Strike** - Temporarily stun opponent
- **Flurry of Blows** - Multiple rapid punches/kicks
- **Improved Unarmed Damage** - Fists count as weapons

### Advanced Techniques
- **Grappling Master** - Superior wrestling and holds
- **Ki Strike** - Magical force in strikes
- **Deflect Missiles** - Catch arrows/projectiles

### Master Techniques
- **Iron Fist** - Unarmed strikes ignore armor
- **Pressure Points** - Paralyze or disable with precision
- **Quivering Palm** - Death touch technique
