# Social Abilities

Charm, persuasion, and social manipulation.

## Overview

Abilities for influencing others through personality, appearance, and social skills.

**Stat Requirements:** *Presence + Looks/Persuasion/Deception*

## Ability Categories

### Charm & Beauty
- **Charm Person** - Make someone friendly
- **Dazzling Presence** - Distract with appearance
- **Natural Magnetism** - Passive attraction bonus

### Persuasion
- **Silver Tongue** - Extremely persuasive
- **Negotiate Master** - Always get better deals
- **Inspiring Speech** - Boost ally morale

### Deception
- **Master Liar** - Lies are believed
- **Disguise Self** - Change appearance
- **Poker Face** - Unreadable intentions

### Intimidation
- **Intimidating Presence** - Frighten through presence
- **Menacing Glare** - Stare down opponents
- **Terrifying Reputation** - Fear precedes you

### Mass Influence
- **Mass Charm** - Charm multiple people
- **Enthrall** - Captivate entire audience
- **Overwhelming Presence** - Dominate social situations

### Master Techniques
- **Cult of Personality** - Inspire fanatical loyalty
- **Social Puppet** - Control someone's actions
- **Legendary Presence** - Mythical social power
