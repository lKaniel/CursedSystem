# Bows & Crossbows

Ranged precision and long-distance combat.

## Overview

Ranged weapons for attacking from afar with accuracy and power.

**Stat Requirements:** *Agility + Ranged Combat*

## Weapon Types

### Bows
- Shortbow
- Longbow
- Composite Bow
- Recurve Bow

### Crossbows
- Light Crossbow
- Heavy Crossbow
- Hand Crossbow
- Repeating Crossbow

## Skill Progression

### Tier 0: Proficiency
**[[Bow Proficiency]]**
- Prerequisites: Agility 4
- Use bows and crossbows effectively

### Tier 1: Basic Techniques
**[[Aimed Shot]]**
- Prerequisites: [[Bow Proficiency]], Agility 8, Ranged Combat 6
- Attack with advantage, bonus damage

### Tier 2: Advanced Techniques
**[[Rapid Fire]]**
- Prerequisites: [[Aimed Shot]], Agility 12, Ranged Combat 10
- 3 quick shots in succession

**[[Piercing Shot]]**
- Prerequisites: [[Aimed Shot]], Agility 12, Ranged Combat 10
- Arrow pierces through multiple targets

### Tier 3: Expert Techniques
**[[Volley]]**
- Prerequisites: [[Rapid Fire]] OR [[Piercing Shot]], Agility 16, Ranged Combat 14
- Rain arrows over 20-foot area

**[[Headshot]]**
- Prerequisites: [[Volley]], Agility 18, Ranged Combat 18
- Precision strike for massive damage/instant kill

### Tier 4: Master Techniques
**[[Arrow Storm]]**
- Prerequisites: [[Headshot]], Agility 20, Ranged Combat 20
- Massive 40-foot area bombardment
