# Axes & Hammers

Crushing and cleaving weapon mastery.

## Overview

Heavy, brutal weapons that excel at raw damage and breaking through defenses.

**Stat Requirements:** *Might + Heavy Melee*

## Weapon Types

### Axes
- Battleaxe
- Greataxe
- Hatchet
- War Axe

### Hammers
- Warhammer
- Maul
- Mace
- Morningstar

## Skill Progression

### Tier 0: Proficiency
**[[Axe and Hammer Proficiency]]**
- Prerequisites: Might 6
- Basic competence with crushing weapons

### Tier 1: Basic Techniques
**[[Power Attack]]**
- Prerequisites: [[Axe and Hammer Proficiency]], Might 8, Heavy Melee 6
- Trade accuracy for extra damage

### Tier 2: Advanced Techniques
**[[Cleave]]**
- Prerequisites: [[Power Attack]], Might 12, Heavy Melee 10
- Strike through one enemy to hit another

**[[Crushing Blow]]**
- Prerequisites: [[Cleave]], Might 14, Heavy Melee 12
- Overhead strike with stun effect

### Tier 3: Expert Techniques
**[[Skull Splitter]]**
- Prerequisites: [[Crushing Blow]], Might 18, Heavy Melee 16
- Devastating single-target massive damage

### Tier 4: Master Techniques
**[[Earthquake Slam]]**
- Prerequisites: [[Skull Splitter]], Might 20, Heavy Melee 20, Endurance 12
- Ground slam AoE, knocks enemies prone
