# Innate Magic (Sorcerer)

Natural spellcasting - magic you know instinctively.

## Overview

Innate Magic represents spells you **KNOW** - they are part of you and can be cast freely without needing books or patrons.

**Stat Requirements:** *Intellect + Arcana OR Awareness + Survival*

## How It Works

- Spells are **remembered/known** permanently once learned
- No spellbook required
- Cast using Mana
- Can combine arcane and nature magic

## Spell Categories

### Arcane Spells (Intellect + Arcana)
- Fireball
- Misty Step
- Lightning Bolt
- Magic Missile
- Polymorph
- Teleport

### Nature Spells (Awareness + Survival)
- Entangle
- Goodberry
- Wildshape
- Pass Without Trace
- Wall of Thorns
- Commune with Nature
