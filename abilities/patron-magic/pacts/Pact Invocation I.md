# Pact Invocation I

## Description
Your patron grants you a permanent boon - a fragment of their power that manifests as an always-active ability.

## Prerequisites
- **Level:** 3+
- Must have: [[Pact Initiate]]

## Details
- **Type:** Passive Ability
- **Category:** [[Patron Magic]] - Invocation
- **Cost:** 1 Ability Point
- **Cooldown:** N/A (Permanent)

## Effect
Choose one invocation from the available list. This invocation is permanently active and costs no resources to use.

### Maximum Invocations
You can have up to **(Presence/4) + (Awareness/4)** invocations active simultaneously.
- Presence 12, Awareness 8: 3 + 2 = **5 max invocations**
- At high levels (40+ stats): 10+ invocations possible

### Available Invocations

**Universal Invocations (Any Patron):**
- [[Eldritch Sight]] - Detect magic at will, see invisible creatures
- [[Devil's Tongue]] - Expertise (double bonus) in Persuasion and Deception
- [[Armor of Shadows]] - Mage Armor always active (13 + Agility/2 AC)
- [[Beast Speech]] - Speak with animals at will
- [[Eyes of the Rune Keeper]] - Read all writing and languages
- [[Mask of Many Faces]] - Change appearance at will (illusion)
- [[Whispers of the Grave]] - Speak with dead (1/day)

**Patron-Specific Invocations:**
See your patron type for exclusive invocations:
- [[Celestial Invocations]]
- [[Fiend Invocations]]
- [[Archfey Invocations]]
- [[Eldritch Invocations]]
- [[Undying Invocations]]
- [[Primordial Invocations]]

## Changing Invocations
You can swap one invocation when you:
- Gain a level
- Complete a major quest for your patron
- Undergo a significant character arc (DM discretion)

## Scaling
- Additional invocation slots unlock through:
  - [[Pact Invocation II]] at level 7
  - [[Pact Invocation III]] at level 13
  - [[Pact Invocation IV]] at level 19
- Some invocations become more powerful as your stats increase

## Special
- Invocations don't require concentration
- Work even if you're out of pact slots
- Can't be dispelled (they're part of you)
- If pact breaks, you lose all invocations

## Progression
- **Requires:** [[Pact Initiate]]
- **Unlocks:** [[Pact Invocation II]] (at level 7+)
- **Synergizes with:** All patron abilities
