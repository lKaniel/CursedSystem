# Pact Initiate

## Description
Form a sacred oath or dark bargain with a powerful otherworldly entity, gaining access to their magic in exchange for service.

## Prerequisites
- **Presence:** 10 OR **Awareness:** 10
- **One of:** Insight 8, Deception 8, Performance 8, or Survival 8

## Details
- **Type:** Foundational Ability
- **Category:** [[Patron Magic]] - Core
- **Cost:** 1 Ability Point
- **Cooldown:** N/A (Permanent)

## Effect
You form a pact with an otherworldly patron, gaining the ability to channel their power.

### Choose Your Patron
Select one patron type (this choice is permanent):
- [[The Celestial]] (Awareness + Insight) - Divine beings of light
- [[The Fiend]] (Presence + Deception) - Infernal powers of darkness
- [[The Archfey]] (Presence + Performance OR Awareness + Survival) - Chaotic fey nobility
- [[The Great Old One]] (Intellect + Arcana OR Awareness + Sixth Sense) - Cosmic horrors
- [[The Undying]] (Intellect + Arcana OR Endurance + Fortitude) - Immortal undead lords
- [[The Primordial]] (Might + Athletics OR Awareness + Survival) - Elemental titans

### Pact Slots
You gain **2 Pact Slots** that recharge on a Long Rest.
- Spend 1 pact slot to cast any patron spell you know
- All spells cast at your current power level
- No mana cost (only pact slot + SP)

**Scaling:**
- Level 1-2: 2 pact slots
- Level 3-5: 3 pact slots
- Level 6-8: 4 pact slots
- Level 9-11: 5 pact slots
- Level 12-14: 6 pact slots
- Level 15+: +1 slot every 3 levels

### Starting Spells
Learn 3 spells from your patron's spell list (Tier 1-2 spells only).

### Pact Weapon (Optional)
If your patron allows, you can summon a magical weapon:
- Costs 1 SP to summon/dismiss (free action)
- Counts as magical for overcoming resistance
- Uses Presence OR Awareness for attack rolls
- Damage: 1d8 + (Presence/2 or Awareness/2)
- Can be any melee weapon form you choose

## Scaling
- At Level 6: Learn 2 more patron spells (Tier 2-3)
- At Level 12: Learn 2 more patron spells (Tier 3-4)
- At Level 18: Learn 2 more patron spells (Tier 4-5)
- Pact weapon damage increases by 1 die at levels 10, 15, 20

## Special: Patron Obligations
Your patron expects service. Failure to uphold your obligations can result in:
- Loss of temporary pact slots
- Disadvantage on patron spell attacks
- Losing invocations
- Full pact break (lose all abilities)

Each patron has different expectations - see patron type for details.

## Progression
- **Unlocks:** [[Pact Invocation I]], [[Expanded Spell List]], [[Pact Recovery]]
- **Required for:** All other Patron Magic abilities
