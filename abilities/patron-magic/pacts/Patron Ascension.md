# Patron Ascension

## Description
Merge your essence with your patron's power, becoming an avatar of their will made flesh.

## Prerequisites
- **Level:** 18+
- Must have: [[Pact Initiate]], [[Pact Invocation III]], [[Patron's Champion]]
- **Presence:** 20+ OR **Awareness:** 20+
- **Patron relationship:** Excellent standing

## Details
- **Type:** Transformation Ability
- **Category:** [[Patron Magic]] - Legendary
- **Cost:** 1 Ability Point
- **Cooldown:** Once per Long Rest

## Effect
As an action (costs 5 SP), transform into your patron's avatar form for up to 1 hour.

### Universal Avatar Benefits
- **+50 Temporary HP** (scales with level)
- **Resistance to 2 damage types** (based on patron)
- **+5 to AC**
- **Flight speed 60 feet** (or other movement based on patron)
- **Aura (30 feet):** Allies gain advantage on saves
- **Enhanced Spells:** Pact spells cost 0 SP and deal maximum damage
- **Intimidating Presence:** Enemies within 30 feet must save or be frightened

### Patron-Specific Forms

**Celestial Avatar:**
- Angelic appearance: wings, halo, radiant aura
- Resistance: Radiant, Necrotic
- **Radiant Smite:** Melee attacks deal +3d8 radiant damage
- **Healing Aura:** Allies in aura regain (Awareness/2) HP per round
- **Turn the Unholy:** Undead and fiends must flee (Awareness save DC)

**Fiend Avatar:**
- Demonic/devilish appearance: horns, wings, flames
- Resistance: Fire, Poison
- **Hellfire Touch:** Melee attacks deal +3d8 fire damage
- **Fear Aura:** Enemies in aura have disadvantage on attacks
- **Infernal Command:** Command one creature (Presence save DC)

**Archfey Avatar:**
- Fey beauty/terror: glowing skin, nature elements, otherworldly
- Resistance: Psychic, Poison
- **Charm Aura:** Enemies have disadvantage on saves vs charm
- **Misty Form:** Can teleport 30 feet as bonus action
- **Nature's Wrath:** Summon vines/thorns (3d10 damage, restrain)

**Great Old One Avatar:**
- Eldritch horror: tentacles, extra eyes, reality distortion
- Resistance: Psychic, Cold
- **Mind Blast:** 60-foot cone, 4d8 psychic damage, stunned (Int save)
- **Maddening Aura:** Enemies in aura must save or have disadvantage
- **Void Touch:** Touch attack, 3d10 psychic + banish to another plane (1 round)

**Undying Avatar:**
- Undead appearance: spectral, skeletal, vampiric
- Resistance: Necrotic, Poison (immune to poison condition)
- **Life Drain:** Melee attacks drain life, heal you for half damage dealt
- **Undead Servitude:** Raise dead within 30 feet as zombies (3d4 zombies)
- **Death Ward:** If reduced to 0 HP, drop to 1 HP instead (once)

**Primordial Avatar:**
- Elemental form: made of fire, stone, storm, ice
- Resistance: Two elemental types (choose when transforming)
- **Elemental Fury:** All attacks deal +2d10 elemental damage
- **Environmental Control:** Weather/terrain within 60 feet bends to your will
- **Elemental Strike:** Ranged attack (120 feet), 5d10 elemental damage

## Scaling
- At Level 20: Can use **twice per long rest**
- At Presence/Awareness 25+: Duration increases to 2 hours
- At Presence/Awareness 30+: Temp HP increases to 75
- At Presence/Awareness 35+: Can transform as bonus action (2 SP)

## Special
**While Transformed:**
- You maintain your mental stats
- Your physical appearance changes dramatically
- NPCs will react with awe, fear, or worship
- Enemies may flee rather than fight
- You radiate your patron's aura visibly

**Drawbacks:**
- After transformation ends, gain 1 level of exhaustion
- Cannot transform again until long rest
- If knocked unconscious, transformation ends immediately

## Lore
"To become your patron's avatar is to walk the line between mortal and divine. You are no longer merely channeling their power - you ARE their power, manifest in the material world. Few mortals achieve this level of communion, and fewer still retain their sanity."
