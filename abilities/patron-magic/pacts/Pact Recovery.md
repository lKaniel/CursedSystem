# Pact Recovery

## Description
Deepen your connection to your patron, allowing you to draw upon their power even during brief moments of rest.

## Prerequisites
- **Level:** 5+
- Must have: [[Pact Initiate]]
- **Presence:** 12 OR **Awareness:** 12

## Details
- **Type:** Passive Ability
- **Category:** [[Patron Magic]] - Core
- **Cost:** 1 Ability Point
- **Cooldown:** N/A (Permanent)

## Effect
Once per day, you can regain **1 Pact Slot** during a Short Rest (10 minutes).

### Requirements During Rest
To regain the slot, you must actively honor your patron:
- **Celestial:** Pray, meditate, or perform healing
- **Fiend:** Make an offering, inflict suffering, or plot schemes
- **Archfey:** Dance, sing, tell stories, or frolic in nature
- **Great Old One:** Meditate on cosmic horrors, read forbidden texts
- **Undying:** Drain life energy, commune with undead, resist mortality
- **Primordial:** Attune to elemental forces, meditate in natural settings

### Usage
- Can only use **once per day** (resets on long rest)
- Requires 10+ minutes of uninterrupted rest
- Must actively engage with patron (not just passive rest)
- Does NOT work if patron is displeased with you

## Scaling
- At Level 10: Can use **twice per day**
- At Level 15: Can use **three times per day**
- At Level 20: Regain **2 pact slots** per short rest (can use 3 times)

## Special
This ability represents your growing mastery over the pact bond. You're learning to draw power more efficiently from your patron.

**Roleplay Opportunity:**
- Short rests become moments to engage with your patron
- Visions, omens, or direct communication may occur
- Other party members may witness your rituals
- Great for building character and world lore

## Tactics
**Resource Management:**
- Use pact slots liberally in first encounter
- Short rest to regain slots before next combat
- Balances pact magic's limited slots with more frequent access

**Multiple Short Rests:**
At high levels, you can do 3 short rests per day:
- Morning rest: +2 slots
- Midday rest: +2 slots
- Evening rest: +2 slots
- Total: +6 pact slots beyond your maximum!

## Progression
- **Requires:** [[Pact Initiate]]
- **Unlocks:** [[Greater Pact Slots]]
- **Synergizes with:** [[Pact Invocation I]], spell-heavy builds
