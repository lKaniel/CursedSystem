# Pike Wall

Create an impassable barrier of polearms.

## Type
Weapon Technique - Action/Stance

## Prerequisites
- **Previous Skill:** [[Impale]]
- **Stats Required:** Might 18, Heavy Melee 16

## Effect
Plant your polearm and create a defensive zone that enemies cannot easily cross.

**Cost:** 10 SP (activation)
**Duration:** Until you move or are knocked prone
**Effect:**
- Create a 15-foot line extending from you
- Any enemy trying to cross this line must make Agility save (DC = 10 + Might/2 + Heavy Melee/2) or take 2d10 + Might damage and have their movement stopped
- If they save: Take half damage, can continue moving
- Allies can pass through freely

**Drawback:** You cannot move while maintaining Pike Wall

## Example
You activate Pike Wall (10 SP). Three enemies charge. Each must save vs DC 26 or take 2d10 + 18 damage and stop moving.

## Scaling
Save DC and damage scale with Might and Heavy Melee.
