# Blade Mastery

Enhanced damage and accuracy with swords.

## Type
Weapon Technique - Passive

## Prerequisites
- **Previous Skill:** [[Sword Proficiency]]
- **Stats Required:** Might 8 OR Agility 8, Relevant Melee Skill 6

## Effect
You've mastered the art of sword fighting.

- **+2 to hit** with all sword attacks
- **+1 damage** per 5 points in your attack stat (Might for heavy swords, Agility for light swords)
- Critical hits on 19-20 instead of just 20

## Cost
1 Ability Point

## Scaling
Damage bonus increases as your stats grow.

## Unlocks
- [[Riposte]]
- [[Power Slash]]
