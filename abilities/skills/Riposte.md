# Riposte

Counter-attack after successfully parrying.

## Type
Weapon Technique - Reaction

## Prerequisites
- **Previous Skill:** [[Blade Mastery]]
- **Stats Required:** Agility 10, Light Melee 8

## Effect
When an enemy misses you with a melee attack, you can strike back.

**Trigger:** Enemy misses you with melee attack and you have stamina remaining
**Cost:** 3 SP (reaction)
**Attack:** Make immediate sword attack against attacker
**Damage:** Normal weapon damage + (Agility/2)

## Example
Enemy swings at you (misses). You still have 8 SP from your turn. You spend 3 SP to Riposte, rolling your sword attack and dealing damage.

## Scaling
Damage scales with Agility and Light Melee.
