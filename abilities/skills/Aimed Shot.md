# Aimed Shot

Take extra time to aim for increased accuracy.

## Type
Weapon Technique - Action

## Prerequisites
- **Previous Skill:** [[Bow Proficiency]]
- **Stats Required:** Agility 8, Ranged Combat 6

## Effect
Carefully line up your shot for improved accuracy.

**Cost:** 5 SP (instead of normal 3 SP)
**Benefit:** Make attack roll with advantage
**Damage:** Normal weapon damage + (Ranged Combat/2)

**Cannot use at close range (within 5 feet)**

## Example
Agility 10, Ranged Combat 8, Longbow (1d8).
Aimed Shot (5 SP): Roll twice, take higher.
Damage: 1d8 + 5 + 4 = 1d8 + 9

## Scaling
Bonus damage scales with Ranged Combat.
