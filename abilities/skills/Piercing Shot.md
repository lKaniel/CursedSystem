# Piercing Shot

Arrow pierces through multiple enemies.

## Type
Weapon Technique - Action

## Prerequisites
- **Previous Skill:** [[Aimed Shot]]
- **Stats Required:** Agility 12, Ranged Combat 10

## Effect
Shoot with such force your arrow continues through targets.

**Cost:** 6 SP
**Range:** Normal weapon range
**Attack:** Make one attack roll
**Effect:** Arrow travels in a line up to 60 feet
- Compare your roll to each enemy's DC in the line
- Each enemy you beat takes full weapon damage
- Can pierce up to 3 enemies

## Example
Three orcs in a line. Piercing Shot (6 SP). Roll 22.
Orc 1 (DC 18): Hit for 1d8 + 6
Orc 2 (DC 20): Hit for 1d8 + 6
Orc 3 (DC 24): Miss
Total: 2d8 + 12 damage split between 2 orcs

## Scaling
Damage scales with Agility.
