# Sweeping Strike

Wide arc attack hitting multiple enemies.

## Type
Weapon Technique - Action

## Prerequisites
- **Previous Skill:** [[Reach Advantage]]
- **Stats Required:** Might 12, Heavy Melee 10

## Effect
Sweep your polearm in a wide arc, hitting all enemies in a line.

**Cost:** 7 SP
**Range:** 10-foot line from you
**Attack:** Make one attack roll, compare to each enemy's DC in the line
**Damage:** Normal weapon damage to each hit enemy

**Note:** Can hit up to 3 enemies if they're positioned in a line

## Example
Three goblins are 5 feet, 10 feet, and 15 feet away in a line. You use Sweeping Strike (7 SP), roll once, compare to all three DCs. Each one you beat takes damage.

## Scaling
Damage scales with Might.
