# Earthquake Slam

Ground-shaking slam that affects an area.

## Type
Weapon Technique - Action

## Prerequisites
- **Previous Skill:** [[Skull Splitter]]
- **Stats Required:** Might 20, Heavy Melee 20, Endurance 12

## Effect
Slam your weapon into the ground with such force the earth trembles.

**Cost:** 15 SP
**Range:** 15-foot radius centered on you
**Effect:**
- All enemies in range must make Agility save (DC = 10 + Might/2 + Heavy Melee/2)
- **Fail:** Take 4d10 + Might bludgeoning damage and fall prone
- **Success:** Take half damage, don't fall prone
- **Difficult Terrain:** Area becomes difficult terrain for 1 minute

**Drawback:** You also fall prone after using this

## Example
Might 22, Heavy Melee 22.
- Area: 15-foot radius
- Save DC: 10 + 11 + 11 = DC 32
- Damage: 4d10 + 22 (avg 44 damage)
- All failed saves fall prone

## Scaling
Save DC and damage scale with Might and Heavy Melee.

## Unlocks
None - This is a master-tier ability.
