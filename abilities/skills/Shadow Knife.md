# Shadow Knife

Teleport to enemy and strike from the shadows.

## Type
Weapon Technique - Action

## Prerequisites
- **Previous Skill:** [[Backstab]]
- **Stats Required:** Agility 16, Light Melee 14, Stealth 12

## Effect
Step through shadows to appear behind your target.

**Cost:** 10 SP + 5 MP
**Range:** 30 feet (must be able to see target)
**Effect:**
- Teleport to space behind target
- Immediately make attack with advantage
- This attack counts as Backstab (if you have it)
- You become hidden after the attack (if there's cover/darkness nearby)

**No Opportunity Attacks:** Teleport doesn't provoke

## Example
Light Melee 16, from 30 feet away. Use Shadow Knife (10 SP + 5 MP). Appear behind enemy. Attack with advantage. Backstab damage: 1d4 + 16d4 + 8. Then hide.

## Scaling
Backstab damage scales with Light Melee.
