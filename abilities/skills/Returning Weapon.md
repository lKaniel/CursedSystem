# Returning Weapon

Enchant thrown weapon to return to your hand.

## Type
Weapon Technique - Toggle/Passive

## Prerequisites
- **Previous Skill:** [[Quick Toss]]
- **Stats Required:** Agility 10, Ranged Combat 8, Arcana 6

## Effect
Your thrown weapons magically return to your hand.

**Cost:** 5 MP (enchantment lasts until long rest)
**Effect:** Any thrown weapon you throw returns to your hand at end of your turn
**Benefit:** Never run out of ammunition
