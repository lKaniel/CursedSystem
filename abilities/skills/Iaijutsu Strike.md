# Iaijutsu Strike

Lightning-fast draw and strike in one motion.

## Type
Weapon Technique - Action

## Prerequisites
- **Previous Skill:** [[Sword Dance]]
- **Stats Required:** Agility 18, Light Melee 16

## Effect
Draw your sword and strike in a single, blindingly fast motion.

**Requirements:** Sword must be sheathed
**Cost:** 6 SP
**Attack:** Make attack roll with advantage
**Damage:** Weapon damage Ã— 2 + (Agility/2) + (Light Melee/2)
**Special:** If this kills the target, you can sheathe your sword as a free action

**Initiative Bonus:** If you use this on your first turn in combat, gain +5 to initiative

## Example
Your katana is sheathed. Combat starts. You use Iaijutsu Strike (6 SP), rolling with advantage. You deal 2d8 + 9 (Agility) + 8 (Light Melee) = 2d8 + 17 damage. The enemy dies. You sheathe your sword (free).

## Scaling
Damage scales heavily with Agility and Light Melee.
