# Crushing Blow

Stun enemies with devastating overhead strikes.

## Type
Weapon Technique - Action

## Prerequisites
- **Previous Skill:** [[Cleave]]
- **Stats Required:** Might 14, Heavy Melee 12

## Effect
Bring your weapon down with bone-crushing force.

**Cost:** 8 SP
**Attack:** Make attack roll with +2 bonus
**Damage:** Normal weapon damage + (Might/2)
**Special:** On hit, target must make Endurance save (DC = 10 + Might/2) or be Stunned until end of their next turn

**Critical Hit:** Target is automatically Stunned, no save

## Example
Might 16, Maul (2d6).
- Attack: +2 bonus to hit
- Damage: 2d6 + 8
- On hit: Target saves vs DC 18 or is Stunned

## Scaling
Save DC and damage scale with Might.
