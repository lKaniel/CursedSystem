# Arrow Storm

Ultimate archery technique - massive area bombardment.

## Type
Weapon Technique - Action

## Prerequisites
- **Previous Skill:** [[Headshot]]
- **Stats Required:** Agility 20, Ranged Combat 20

## Effect
Unleash a devastating storm of arrows covering a huge area.

**Cost:** 20 SP
**Range:** 100 feet
**Area:** 40-foot radius circle
**Ammunition:** Uses 30 arrows
**Effect:**
- All creatures in area must make Agility save (DC = 10 + Agility/2 + Ranged Combat/2)
- **Fail:** Take 8d8 + Agility + Ranged Combat damage
- **Success:** Take half damage
- Area becomes difficult terrain (arrows everywhere)

**Special:** Can use this as a reaction if you have stamina remaining and enemy reinforcements arrive

## Example
Agility 22, Ranged Combat 22.
Arrow Storm on 40-foot area (20 SP). 10 enemies inside.
Save DC: 10 + 11 + 11 = 32
Fail: 8d8 + 44 damage (avg 80 damage!)
Success: 4d8 + 22 damage (avg 40 damage)

## Scaling
Save DC and damage scale massively with stats.

## Unlocks
None - This is a master-tier ability.
