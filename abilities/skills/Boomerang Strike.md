# Boomerang Strike

Weapon hits on the way out AND back.

## Type
Weapon Technique - Action

## Prerequisites
- **Previous Skill:** [[Returning Weapon]]
- **Stats Required:** Agility 16, Ranged Combat 16

## Effect
Throw weapon in an arc that hits twice.

**Requires:** [[Returning Weapon]] active
**Cost:** 10 SP
**Attack:** Make attack roll against target
**On Hit:** Deal damage, weapon arcs back
**Return:** Make second attack roll against same or different target in range
**Both Hits:** Deal double damage total
