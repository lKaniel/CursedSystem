# Skewer

Pierce through multiple enemies in a line.

## Type
Weapon Technique - Action

## Prerequisites
- **Previous Skill:** [[Pike Wall]]
- **Stats Required:** Might 20, Heavy Melee 20

## Effect
Thrust your polearm with such force it pierces through multiple enemies.

**Cost:** 12 SP
**Range:** 20-foot line from you
**Attack:** Make one attack roll with advantage
**Damage:** Weapon damage + (Might) + (Heavy Melee/2) to EACH enemy hit
**Pierce:** Attack continues through enemies up to 20 feet
- First enemy: Full damage
- Second enemy: Full damage if attack beats their DC
- Third enemy: Full damage if attack beats their DC
- etc.

**Special:** Each enemy hit is pushed 5 feet further along the line

## Example
