# Axe and Hammer Proficiency

Basic competence with crushing and chopping weapons.

## Type
Weapon Proficiency - Passive

## Prerequisites
- **Category:** [[Axes and Hammers]]
- **Stats Required:** Might 6

## Effect
You can wield axes and hammers effectively without penalty.

- No disadvantage on axe/hammer attacks
- Use proper damage dice
- Can use weapon special properties

## Weapons Unlocked
- Handaxe (1d6, light, thrown)
- Battleaxe (1d8, versatile)
- Greataxe (1d12, heavy, two-handed)
- Warhammer (1d8, versatile)
- Maul (2d6, heavy, two-handed)

## Scaling
None - this is a basic proficiency.
