# Cleave

Strike through one enemy to hit another behind them.

## Type
Weapon Technique - Action

## Prerequisites
- **Previous Skill:** [[Power Attack]]
- **Stats Required:** Might 12, Heavy Melee 10

## Effect
Your weapon cuts through enemies, hitting multiple foes.

**Cost:** 6 SP
**Requirements:** Two enemies must be adjacent to each other
**Attack:** Make one attack roll against first enemy
**On Hit:**
- Deal normal damage to first enemy
- Make second attack roll against second enemy (same bonus)
- Second attack deals half damage if it hits

## Example
Two orcs stand next to each other. You Cleave (6 SP). First orc: You hit for 15 damage. Second orc: You hit for 7 damage.

## Scaling
Damage scales with Might and weapon choice.
