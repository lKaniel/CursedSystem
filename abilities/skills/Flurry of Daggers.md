# Flurry of Daggers

Unleash a devastating series of rapid strikes.

## Type
Weapon Technique - Action

## Prerequisites
- **Previous Skill:** [[Shadow Knife]] OR [[Dual Wielding]] + Agility 16
- **Stats Required:** Agility 18, Light Melee 18

## Effect
Attack with both weapons in a blur of motion.

**Cost:** 15 SP
**Effect:** Make 6 attacks total (3 main hand, 3 off-hand)
**Attack:** Roll separately for each attack
**Damage:** Each hit deals normal weapon damage

**Special:** If you have [[Quick Strike]], each attack costs 0 SP (already paid 15 SP for the flurry)

## Example
Dual wielding daggers. Use Flurry (15 SP).
Make 6 attack rolls. Each hit deals 1d4 + Agility.
If 5 hit: 5d4 + 35 damage total.

## Scaling
Scales with number of hits and Agility bonus.
