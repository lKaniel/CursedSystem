# Impale

Pin an enemy, dealing ongoing damage.

## Type
Weapon Technique - Action

## Prerequisites
- **Previous Skill:** [[Sweeping Strike]]
- **Stats Required:** Might 14, Heavy Melee 12

## Effect
Thrust your polearm through an enemy, pinning them.

**Cost:** 8 SP
**Attack:** Make attack roll with +2 bonus
**On Hit:**
- Deal normal weapon damage + (Might/2)
- Target is Restrained (speed 0)
- At start of their turn, target takes 1d8 damage automatically
- Target can use action + 4 SP to make Might check (DC = 10 + Might/2) to free themselves

**Your Movement:** You can't move more than 5 feet from impaled target or they become unpinned

## Example
You impale an orc (8 SP). Hit: 1d10 + 7 damage, orc is restrained. Orc's turn: Takes 1d8 damage. Uses action to try to free itself (vs DC 17).

## Scaling
Damage and save DC scale with Might.
