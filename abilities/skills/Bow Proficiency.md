# Bow Proficiency

Basic competence with bows and crossbows.

## Type
Weapon Proficiency - Passive

## Prerequisites
- **Category:** [[Bows and Crossbows]]
- **Stats Required:** Agility 4

## Effect
You can use bows and crossbows effectively without penalty.

- No disadvantage on ranged attacks
- Use proper damage dice
- Understand range increments

## Weapons Unlocked
- Shortbow (1d6, two-handed, range 80/320)
- Longbow (1d8, heavy, two-handed, range 150/600)
- Light Crossbow (1d8, two-handed, loading, range 80/320)
- Heavy Crossbow (1d10, heavy, two-handed, loading, range 100/400)

## Scaling
None - this is a basic proficiency.
