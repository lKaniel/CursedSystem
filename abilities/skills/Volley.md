# Volley

Rain arrows over an area.

## Type
Weapon Technique - Action

## Prerequisites
- **Previous Skill:** [[Rapid Fire]] OR [[Piercing Shot]]
- **Stats Required:** Agility 16, Ranged Combat 14

## Effect
Shoot arrows high into the air to rain down on an area.

**Cost:** 12 SP
**Range:** 60 feet
**Area:** 20-foot radius circle
**Ammunition:** Uses 10 arrows
**Effect:**
- All creatures in area must make Agility save (DC = 10 + Agility/2 + Ranged Combat/2)
- **Fail:** Take 3d8 + (Agility/2) damage
- **Success:** Take half damage

**Cannot use if target area has overhead cover**

## Example
Agility 18, Ranged Combat 16.
Volley on 20-foot area (12 SP). 6 enemies inside.
Save DC: 10 + 9 + 8 = 27
Damage: 3d8 + 9 to those who fail, half to those who succeed.

## Scaling
Save DC and damage scale with stats.
