# Whirling Blade

Spinning attack that hits all adjacent enemies.

## Type
Weapon Technique - Action

## Prerequisites
- **Previous Skill:** [[Riposte]]
- **Stats Required:** Agility 14, Light Melee 12 OR Might 14, Heavy Melee 12

## Effect
Spin in a circle, striking all enemies within 5 feet.

**Cost:** 8 SP
**Range:** 5 feet (all adjacent enemies)
**Attack:** Make one attack roll, compare to each enemy's DC
**Damage:** Normal weapon damage to each hit

**Note:** This counts as ONE attack action, not multiple.

## Example
You're surrounded by 4 goblins. You use Whirling Blade (8 SP), roll d(stat) + stat once, and compare it to each goblin's DC. Each one you beat takes full weapon damage.

## Scaling
- Damage scales with your attack stat
- Number of targets limited only by positioning
