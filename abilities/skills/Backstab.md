# Backstab

Massive bonus damage when attacking from behind or from stealth.

## Type
Weapon Technique - Passive

## Prerequisites
- **Previous Skill:** [[Quick Strike]]
- **Stats Required:** Agility 14, Light Melee 12, Stealth 8

## Effect
You know exactly where to strike for maximum damage.

**Requirements:** Attack from behind enemy OR attack while hidden
**Bonus Damage:** +(Light Melee) extra damage dice
**Example:** Light Melee 12 = +12d4 damage with daggers!

**Critical Hit:** Double ALL damage dice (including backstab dice)

## Example
Light Melee 14, Dagger (1d4), attacking from behind.
- Normal: 1d4 + 7
- Backstab: 1d4 + 14d4 + 7 = 15d4 + 7 damage!

## Scaling
Scales dramatically with Light Melee - each point adds another damage die.
