# Perfect Aim

Never miss with thrown weapons.

## Type
Weapon Technique - Passive/Active

## Prerequisites
- **Previous Skill:** [[Multi-Throw]] OR [[Boomerang Strike]]
- **Stats Required:** Agility 20 OR Might 20, Ranged Combat 20

## Effect
Master-level throwing accuracy.

**Passive:** +4 to hit with all thrown weapons
**Active (once per long rest):** Declare one thrown attack to automatically hit (still roll damage)
**Critical:** Critical hit range with thrown weapons: 18-20

## Unlocks
None - This is a master-tier ability.
