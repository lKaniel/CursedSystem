# Headshot

Precision strike to the head for massive damage or instant kill.

## Type
Weapon Technique - Action

## Prerequisites
- **Previous Skill:** [[Volley]]
- **Stats Required:** Agility 18, Ranged Combat 18

## Effect
Target the head with lethal precision.

**Cost:** 10 SP
**Attack:** Make attack roll at -2 penalty (called shot)
**On Hit:**
- Deal triple weapon damage
- Target must make Endurance save (DC = 10 + Agility/2 + Ranged Combat/2)
- **Fail:** If target has less than 30 HP, instant kill. Otherwise, stunned for 1 round
- **Success:** Takes triple damage, no additional effect

**Critical Hit:** Automatic instant kill if target under 50 HP

## Example
Agility 20, Ranged Combat 20, Longbow (1d8).
Headshot (10 SP): Attack at -2.
Hit: 3d8 + 10 damage
Save DC 30 or: Under 30 HP = death, Over 30 HP = stunned

## Scaling
Save DC and damage scale with stats.
