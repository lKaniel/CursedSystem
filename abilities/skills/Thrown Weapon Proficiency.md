# Thrown Weapon Proficiency

Basic competence with thrown weapons.

## Type
Weapon Proficiency - Passive

## Prerequisites
- **Category:** [[Thrown Weapons]]
- **Stats Required:** Agility 4 OR Might 4

## Effect
You can throw weapons effectively without penalty.

## Weapons Unlocked
- Throwing Dagger (1d4, finesse, thrown 20/60)
- Throwing Axe (1d6, thrown 20/60)
- Javelin (1d6, thrown 30/120)
- Chakram (1d6, finesse, thrown 30/90)
