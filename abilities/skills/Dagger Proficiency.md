# Dagger Proficiency

Basic competence with light blades and daggers.

## Type
Weapon Proficiency - Passive

## Prerequisites
- **Category:** [[Daggers and Light Blades]]
- **Stats Required:** Agility 4

## Effect
You can wield daggers and light blades effectively without penalty.

- No disadvantage on dagger attacks
- Use proper damage dice
- Can dual wield light weapons

## Weapons Unlocked
- Dagger (1d4, finesse, light, thrown)
- Stiletto (1d4, finesse, light)
- Kukri (1d4, finesse, light)
- Shortsword (1d6, finesse, light)

## Scaling
None - this is a basic proficiency.
