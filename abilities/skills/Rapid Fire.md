# Rapid Fire

Shoot multiple arrows in quick succession.

## Type
Weapon Technique - Action

## Prerequisites
- **Previous Skill:** [[Aimed Shot]]
- **Stats Required:** Agility 12, Ranged Combat 10

## Effect
Loose multiple arrows rapidly.

**Cost:** 8 SP
**Effect:** Make 3 ranged attacks against one or multiple targets
**Attacks:** Roll separately for each attack
**Damage:** Normal weapon damage for each hit
**Ammunition:** Uses 3 arrows/bolts

**Special:** All attacks must be made against targets within 30 feet

## Example
You have 3 goblins at 20 feet. Rapid Fire (8 SP).
Attack 1: Hit for 1d8 + 6
Attack 2: Hit for 1d8 + 6
Attack 3: Miss
Total: 2d8 + 12 damage

## Scaling
Damage per hit scales with Agility.
