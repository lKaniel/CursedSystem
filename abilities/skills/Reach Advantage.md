# Reach Advantage

Master the use of reach to control space.

## Type
Weapon Technique - Passive

## Prerequisites
- **Previous Skill:** [[Polearm Proficiency]]
- **Stats Required:** Might 8, Heavy Melee 6

## Effect
You excel at keeping enemies at bay with your reach.

**Passive Benefits:**
- Opportunity attacks trigger when enemies enter your 10-foot reach (not just when they leave)
- +1 to AC against enemies who don't have reach weapons
- Can make attacks of opportunity even if enemy uses Disengage

## Example
Enemy 15 feet away moves toward you. When they enter 10 feet: Opportunity attack. They continue and attack you: You have +1 AC against them.

## Scaling
None - this is a tactical ability.
