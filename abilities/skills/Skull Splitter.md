# Skull Splitter

Devastating single-target attack with massive damage.

## Type
Weapon Technique - Action

## Prerequisites
- **Previous Skill:** [[Crushing Blow]]
- **Stats Required:** Might 18, Heavy Melee 16

## Effect
Channel all your strength into one apocalyptic strike.

**Cost:** 10 SP
**Attack:** Make attack roll
**Damage:** Weapon damage × 3 + (Might) + (Heavy Melee/2)
**Critical:** Damage × 2 (this stacks - weapon dice become × 6)

**Drawback:** After using Skull Splitter, your next attack has disadvantage (from exhaustion)

## Example
Might 20, Heavy Melee 18, Greataxe (1d12).
- Normal: 1d12 + 10
- Skull Splitter: 3d12 + 20 + 9 = 3d12 + 29 damage!
- On crit: 6d12 + 29 damage

## Scaling
Scales massively with both Might and Heavy Melee.
