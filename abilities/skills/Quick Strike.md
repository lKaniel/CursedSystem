# Quick Strike

Lightning-fast attack with reduced cost.

## Type
Weapon Technique - Action

## Prerequisites
- **Previous Skill:** [[Dual Wielding]]
- **Stats Required:** Agility 12, Light Melee 10

## Effect
Strike with blinding speed.

**Cost:** 2 SP (instead of normal 3 SP for light attack)
**Attack:** Normal attack roll
**Damage:** Normal weapon damage + (Agility/2)
**Limitation:** Can only use with light melee weapons

**Synergy:** Combines well with Dual Wielding for very cheap double attacks

## Example
Agility 14.
Quick Strike with main hand (2 SP): 1d4 + 7 damage
Dual Wield off-hand (2 SP): 1d4 + 3 damage
Total: 4 SP for two attacks

## Scaling
Damage scales with Agility.
