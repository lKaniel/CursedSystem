# Dual Wielding

Fight effectively with a weapon in each hand.

## Type
Weapon Technique - Passive

## Prerequisites
- **Previous Skill:** [[Dagger Proficiency]]
- **Stats Required:** Agility 8, Light Melee 6

## Effect
Master two-weapon fighting.

**Passive Benefits:**
- Can wield two light weapons without penalty
- When attacking with main hand, can immediately attack with off-hand for only 2 SP (instead of full 3 SP)
- Add half your Agility bonus to off-hand damage

## Example
Main hand dagger attack (3 SP): 1d4 + 7 damage
Off-hand dagger attack (2 SP): 1d4 + 3 damage
Total: 5 SP for two attacks

## Scaling
Bonus damage scales with Agility.
