# Power Attack

Trade accuracy for devastating damage.

## Type
Weapon Technique - Toggle

## Prerequisites
- **Previous Skill:** [[Axe and Hammer Proficiency]]
- **Stats Required:** Might 8, Heavy Melee 6

## Effect
Wind up for massive damage at the cost of accuracy.

**Activation:** Declare before making attack
**Effect:**
- Take -2 penalty to attack roll
- Add +(Might/2) extra damage on hit
- Works with any Heavy Melee weapon

**No Additional Cost:** This modifies your attack, doesn't cost extra SP

## Example
Might 14, wielding Greataxe (1d12).
- Normal attack: d12 + 7 damage
- Power Attack: -2 to hit, but d12 + 14 damage on hit

## Scaling
Bonus damage scales with Might.
