# Sword Dance

Fluid fighting style granting bonus actions.

## Type
Combat Stance - Toggle

## Prerequisites
- **Previous Skill:** [[Whirling Blade]]
- **Stats Required:** Agility 16, Light Melee 14, Acrobatics 10

## Effect
Enter a graceful combat stance that reduces action costs.

**Activation:** 2 SP (at start of turn)
**Duration:** Until end of turn
**Benefits:**
- Movement costs reduced by 1 SP per 10 feet (minimum 1 SP per 10 feet)
- Light Melee attacks cost 2 SP instead of 3 SP
- Opportunity attacks against you have disadvantage

**Drawback:** Cannot use Heavy Melee weapons while in Sword Dance

## Example
Activate Sword Dance (2 SP). Move 20 feet (2 SP instead of 4 SP). Attack three times (6 SP instead of 9 SP). Total: 10 SP for movement + 3 attacks.

## Scaling
Benefits improve with Agility and Light Melee.
