# Multi-Throw

Throw multiple weapons at once.

## Type
Weapon Technique - Action

## Prerequisites
- **Previous Skill:** [[Quick Toss]]
- **Stats Required:** Agility 14 OR Might 14, Ranged Combat 12

## Effect
Throw several weapons simultaneously.

**Cost:** 8 SP
**Effect:** Make 4 thrown weapon attacks (can split between targets)
**Ammunition:** Uses 4 thrown weapons
