# Vital Strike

Target vital weak points for potential instant kill.

## Type
Weapon Technique - Action

## Prerequisites
- **Previous Skill:** [[Flurry of Daggers]]
- **Stats Required:** Agility 20, Light Melee 20, Stealth 16

## Effect
Strike with surgical precision at vital organs.

**Requirements:** Target must be surprised, stunned, paralyzed, or you're hidden
**Cost:** 12 SP
**Attack:** Make attack roll with advantage
**On Hit:**
- Deal triple weapon damage + Backstab damage (if applicable)
- Target must make Endurance save (DC = 10 + Agility/2 + Light Melee/2)
- **Fail:** Target drops to 0 HP (instant kill if HP < 50)
- **Success:** Takes damage normally

**Boss/Elite Immunity:** Creatures with legendary actions are immune to instant kill, but still take triple damage

## Example
Agility 22, Light Melee 22. Attack paralyzed enemy.
Vital Strike (12 SP): Attack with advantage.
Hit: 3d4 + 22d4 (backstab) + 11 = 25d4 + 11 damage
Save DC 32 or die (if under 50 HP)

## Scaling
Save DC and damage scale with both stats.

## Unlocks
None - This is a master-tier ability.
